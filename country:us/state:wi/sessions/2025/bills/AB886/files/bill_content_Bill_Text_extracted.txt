Referred to Committee on Mental Health and Substance Abuse Prevention.AB886,1,21An Act to create 20.435 (5) (eh) of the statutes; relating to: children’s mental 2health treatment grants and making an appropriation.Analysis by the Legislative Reference BureauThis bill requires the Department of Health Services to award grants of up to $50,000 to cities, villages, towns, counties, and nonprofit organizations to increase children’s access to research-based mental health treatments other than medication or talk therapy. The bill appropriates $2,000,000 for the grants. The grant program ends two years after the effective date of the bill.For further information see the state and local fiscal estimate, which will be printed as an appendix to this bill.The people of the state of Wisconsin, represented in senate and assembly, do enact as follows:AB886,13Section 1. 20.005 (3) (schedule) of the statutes: at the appropriate place, 4insert the following amounts for the purposes indicated: - See PDF for table AB886,26Section 2. 20.435 (5) (eh) of the statutes is created to read:AB886,2,9720.435 (5) (eh) Children’s mental health treatment grants pilot program.
