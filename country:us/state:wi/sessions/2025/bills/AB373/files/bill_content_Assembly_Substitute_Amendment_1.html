ASSEMBLY SUBSTITUTE AMENDMENT 1,
TO ASSEMBLY BILL 373
February 17, 2026 - Offered by Representative Brill.
AB373-ASA1,1,3
1An Act to create 71.07 (8n) and 71.10 (4) (ct) of the statutes; relating to:
2creating a nonrefundable individual income tax credit for the parent of a
3stillbirth.
The people of the state of Wisconsin, represented in senate and assembly, do enact as follows:
AB373-ASA1,14Section 1. 71.07 (8n) of the statutes is created to read:
AB373-ASA1,1,5571.07 (8n) Stillbirth tax credit. (a) Definitions. In this subsection:
AB373-ASA1,1,761. “Claimant” means the parent of a stillbirth who files a claim under this
7subsection.
AB373-ASA1,1,982. “Stillbirth” means a birth that occurs in this state that results in a stillbirth
9for which a fetal death report is required under s. 69.18 (1) (e) 1.
AB373-ASA1,2,310(b) Filing claims. Subject to the limitations and conditions provided in this
1subsection, a claimant may claim as a credit against the tax imposed under s. 71.02,
2up to the amount of the tax, $2,000 for the taxable year in which the stillbirth
3occurs.
AB373-ASA1,2,54(c) Limitations and conditions. 1. No credit may be claimed under this
5subsection by a part-year resident or a nonresident of this state.
AB373-ASA1,2,862. No credit may be allowed under this subsection for a taxable year covering
7a period of less than 12 months, except for a taxable year closed by reason of the
8death of the taxpayer.
AB373-ASA1,2,1093. No credit may be allowed under this subsection unless it is claimed within
10the period specified in s. 71.75 (2).
AB373-ASA1,2,12114. If a married couple files a joint return, the maximum amount that may be
12claimed by the couple under this subsection is $2,000 for each stillbirth.
AB373-ASA1,2,14135. If a married couple files separate returns, the maximum amount that may
14be claimed by each spouse under this subsection is $1,000 for each stillbirth.
AB373-ASA1,2,16156. If the parents of a stillbirth are unmarried, the maximum amount that may
16be claimed by each parent under this subsection is $1,000 for each stillbirth.
AB373-ASA1,2,19177. To claim the credit under this subsection, a claimant shall submit, with his
18or her return for the taxable year to which the claim relates, a copy of the fetal death
19report relating to the stillbirth for which the credit is claimed.
AB373-ASA1,2,2120(d) Administration. Subsection (9e) (d), to the extent that it applies to the
21credit under that subsection, applies to the credit under this subsection.
AB373-ASA1,222Section 2. 71.10 (4) (ct) of the statutes is created to read:
AB373-ASA1,2,232371.10 (4) (ct) Stillbirth tax credit under s. 71.07 (8n).
AB373-ASA1,3
1Section 3. Initial applicability.
AB373-ASA1,3,52(1) This act first applies to taxable years beginning on January 1 of the year in
3which this subsection takes effect, except that if this subsection takes effect after
4July 31, this act first applies to taxable years beginning on January 1 of the year
5following the year in which this subsection takes effect.
AB373-ASA1,3,66(end)
