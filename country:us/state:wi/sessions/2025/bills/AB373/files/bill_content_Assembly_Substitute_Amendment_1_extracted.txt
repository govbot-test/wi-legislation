(a) Definitions. In this subsection:AB373-ASA1,1,761. “Claimant” means the parent of a stillbirth who files a claim under this 7subsection.AB373-ASA1,1,982. “Stillbirth” means a birth that occurs in this state that results in a stillbirth 9for which a fetal death report is required under s. 69.18 (1) (e) 1.AB373-ASA1,2,310(b) Filing claims. Subject to the limitations and conditions provided in this 1subsection, a claimant may claim as a credit against the tax imposed under s. 71.02, 2up to the amount of the tax, $2,000 for the taxable year in which the stillbirth 3occurs.AB373-ASA1,2,54(c) Limitations and conditions. 1. No credit may be claimed under this 5subsection by a part-year resident or a nonresident of this state.AB373-ASA1,2,862. No credit may be allowed under this subsection for a taxable year covering 7a period of less than 12 months, except for a taxable year closed by reason of the 8death of the taxpayer.AB373-ASA1,2,1093. No credit may be allowed under this subsection unless it is claimed within 10the period specified in s. 71.75 (2).AB373-ASA1,2,12114. If a married couple files a joint return, the maximum amount that may be 12claimed by the couple under this subsection is $2,000 for each stillbirth.AB373-ASA1,2,14135. If a married couple files separate returns, the maximum amount that may 14be claimed by each spouse under this subsection is $1,000 for each stillbirth.AB373-ASA1,2,16156. If the parents of a stillbirth are unmarried, the maximum amount that may 16be claimed by each parent under this subsection is $1,000 for each stillbirth.AB373-ASA1,2,19177. To claim the credit under this subsection, a claimant shall submit, with his 18or her return for the taxable year to which the claim relates, a copy of the fetal death 19report relating to the stillbirth for which the credit is claimed.AB373-ASA1,2,2120(d) Administration. Subsection (9e) (d), to the extent that it applies to the 21credit under that subsection, applies to the credit under this subsection.AB373-ASA1,222Section 2. 71.10 (4) (ct) of the statutes is created to read:AB373-ASA1,2,232371.10 (4) (ct) Stillbirth tax credit under s. 71.07 (8n).AB373-ASA1,31Section 3. Initial applicability.AB373-ASA1,3,52(1) This act first applies to taxable years beginning on January 1 of the year in 3which this subsection takes effect, except that if this subsection takes effect after 4July 31, this act first applies to taxable years beginning on January 1 of the year 5following the year in which this subsection takes effect.AB373-ASA1,3,66(end) Down Down /2025/related/amendments/ab373/asa1_ab373 true amends /2025/related/amendments/ab373/asa1_ab373 amends/2025/REG/AB373-ASA1 amends/2025/REG/AB373-ASA1 section true Menu»2025»Related Documents»Amendments»AB373»Amendment ASA1-AB373 × Details for PDF view Link (Permanent link) Bookmark this location View toggle Go to top of document Search in this chapter Search in this section Search in this agency Search in this chapter group Search in this chapter Search in this section Cross references for section Acts affecting this section References to this 1970 Statutes Annotations Appellate Court Citations Administrative Code Index Reference lines Clear highlighting
