Except as provided in subd. 6. c. and, d., and e., all of the private school’s teachers have a teaching license issued by the department or a bachelor’s degree or a degree or educational credential higher than a bachelor’s degree, including a masters or doctorate, from a nationally or regionally accredited institution of higher education.AB518,2Section 2. 118.60 (2) (a) 6. e. of the statutes is created to read:118.60 (2) (a) 6. e. An individual employed by the private school who holds a substitute teacher permit issued by the department under s. 118.19 (7m) may teach at the private school as allowed under the substitute teacher permit.
