Referred to Committee on Campaigns and Elections.AB1175,1,21An Act to amend 5.02 (6m) (f) of the statutes; relating to: student 2identification acceptable for voting.Analysis by the Legislative Reference BureauUnder current law, one of the forms of photographic identification that may be used for voting purposes is a student identification card issued by a university, college, or technical college in Wisconsin that contains an expiration date indicating that the card expires no later than two years after the date of issuance if the individual establishes that he or she is enrolled as a student at the university or college on the date that the card is presented for voting. This bill provides that the student identification card’s expiration date may be up to six years after the date of issuance, rather than two.The people of the state of Wisconsin, represented in senate and assembly, do enact as follows:AB1175,13Section 1. 5.02 (6m) (f) of the statutes is amended to read:AB1175,2,645.02 (6m) (f) An unexpired identification card issued by a university or college 5in this state that is accredited, as defined in s. 39.30 (1) (d), or by a technical college 1in this state that is a member of and governed by the technical college system under 2ch. 38, that contains the date of issuance and signature of the individual to whom it 3is issued and that contains an expiration date indicating that the card expires no 4later than 2 6 years after the date of issuance if the individual establishes that he 5or she is enrolled as a student at the university or college on the date that the card 6is presented.AB1175,2,77(end) Down Down /2025/related/proposals/ab1175 true proposaltext /2025/related/proposals/ab1175 proposaltext/2025/REG/AB1175 proposaltext/2025/REG/AB1175 section true Menu»2025»Related Documents»Proposal Text»AB1175: Bill Text × Details for PDF view Link (Permanent link) Bookmark this location View toggle Go to top of document Search in this chapter Search in this section Search in this agency Search in this chapter group Search in this chapter Search in this section Cross references for section Acts affecting this section References to this 1970 Statutes Annotations Appellate Court Citations Administrative Code Index Reference lines Clear highlighting
