Referred to Committee on Education.AB649,1,41An Act relating to: supplemental funding for whole grade sharing grants, 2supplemental state aid for consolidated school districts, grants for feasibility 3studies of school district consolidation or whole grade sharing, and making an 4appropriation.Analysis by the Legislative Reference BureauThis bill increases the amount appropriated to the Joint Committee on Finance for supplemental appropriations as follows:1. To provide grants to school boards that enter into a whole grade sharing agreement, $2,700,000.2. To provide additional state aid to consolidated school districts if the consolidated school district’s maximum allowable levy rate is greater than the lowest levy rate of the school districts that were consolidated to create the school district, $3,000,000.3. To reimburse school boards for costs incurred for feasibility studies for school district consolidation or a whole grade sharing, $250,000.The bill also requires the Department of Public Instruction to submit a request to JCF to supplement DPI appropriations for the purposes described above.For further information see the state fiscal estimate, which will be printed as an appendix to this bill.The people of the state of Wisconsin, represented in senate and assembly, do enact as follows:AB649,11Section 1. Nonstatutory provisions.AB649,2,62(1) Department of public instruction; request. By no later than July 1, 32026, the department of public instruction shall submit a request to the joint 4committee on finance to supplement from the joint committee on finance 5supplemental appropriation under s. 20.865 (4) (a) a department appropriation for 6each of the purposes described in Section 2 (1) to (3) of this act.AB649,27Section 2. Fiscal changes.AB649,2,118(1) Feasibility studies; grants.
