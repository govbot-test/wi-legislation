2025 ASSEMBLY BILL 704
December 3, 2025 - Introduced by Representatives Green, Brill, Brooks, Dallman, Doyle, B. Jacobson, Joers, Kitchens, Kreibich, McCarville, Melotik, Mursau, O'Connor, Sheehan, Summerfield, Swearingen, VanderMeer and Roe, cosponsored by Senators Tomczyk, Feyen, Marklein, Pfaff and Quinn. Referred to Committee on Forestry, Parks and Outdoor Recreation.
AB704,1,3

2(3j) (bg) 4. of the statutes; relating to: fees for snowmobile registration and
3trail use stickers.
Analysis by the Legislative Reference Bureau
This bill increases the fees for snowmobile registration and trail use stickers. The bill increases the fee for a snowmobile public use registration certificate from $30 to $45. The bill increases fees for a snowmobile trail use sticker as follows:
1. For a snowmobile registered in-state, from $29.25 to $49.25.
2. For a snowmobile registered in-state but owned by a person who is both a member of a snowmobile club and a member of the Association of Wisconsin Snowmobile Clubs, from $9.25 to $19.25.
3. For a snowmobile registered out-of-state, from $49.25 to $69.25.
For further information see the state fiscal estimate, which will be printed as an appendix to this bill.
The people of the state of Wisconsin, represented in senate and assembly, do enact as follows:
