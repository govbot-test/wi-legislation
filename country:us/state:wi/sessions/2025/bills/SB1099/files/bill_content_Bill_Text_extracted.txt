Referred to Committee on Transportation and Local Government.SB1099,1,31An Act to renumber and amend 59.10 (3) (e); to repeal and recreate 59.10 2(1) (d); to create 59.10 (3) (e) 5. of the statutes; relating to: filling vacancies 3on a county board.Analysis by the Legislative Reference BureauUnder current law, in counties other than Milwaukee County, if a vacancy occurs on the county board, the county chairperson, with the approval of the board, appoints a person to fill the vacancy.Under this bill, upon a vacancy in the county board, the board chairperson must publish a public notice of the vacancy and the procedure for applying for the vacant position. No earlier than 14 days after publishing the notice, the board chairperson, with the approval of the board, must appoint a person who applied for the position. The bill also specifies that a vacancy that occurs after November 30 in the year preceding expiration of the term of office may not be filled by such an appointment.For further information see the local fiscal estimate, which will be printed as an appendix to this bill.The people of the state of Wisconsin, represented in senate and assembly, do enact as follows:SB1099,11Section 1. 59.10 (1) (d) of the statutes is repealed and recreated to read:SB1099,2,3259.10 (1) (d) Vacancies. A vacancy on the board shall be filled as provided 3under s. 59.10 (3) (e).SB1099,24Section 2. 59.10 (3) (e) of the statutes is renumbered 59.10 (3) (e) 1. and 5amended to read:SB1099,2,9659.10 (3) (e) 1. If Except as provided under subd. 5., if a vacancy occurs on the 7board, the board chairperson shall publish a class 1 notice, under ch. 985, of the 8vacancy. The notice under this subdivision shall provide the procedure by which a 9person may apply to be considered for the vacant position.SB1099,2,13102.
