2025 ASSEMBLY BILL 949
January 28, 2026 - Introduced by Representatives Doyle, Snodgrass, Ortiz-Velez, DeSmidt, Joers, Stubbs, Stroud, Sinicki and Clancy, cosponsored by Senators Wall and Dassler-Alfheim. Referred to Committee on Insurance.
AB949,1,2
1An Act to create 628.34 (16), 628.349 and 632.105 of the statutes; relating to:
2pet insurance and granting rule-making authority.
Analysis by the Legislative Reference Bureau
This bill generally adopts the provisions in the model Pet Insurance Act approved by the National Association of Insurance Commissioners.
The people of the state of Wisconsin, represented in senate and assembly, do enact as follows:

AB949,1,551. “Pet insurance” has the meaning in s. 632.105 (1) (e).
AB949,1,662. “Pet wellness program” has the meaning in s. 632.105 (1) (f).
AB949,1,77(b) No person may do any of the following:
AB949,1,881. Market a pet wellness program as pet insurance.

AB949,324Section 3. 632.105 of the statutes is created to read:
AB949,4,1
1632.105 Pet insurance. (1) Definitions. In this section:
AB949,4,32(a) “Chronic condition” means a condition that can be treated or managed, but
3not cured.
AB949,4,64(b) “Congenital anomaly or disorder” means a condition that is present from
5birth, whether inherited or caused by the environment, and that may cause or
6contribute to illness or disease.
AB949,4,87(c) “Hereditary disorder” means an abnormality that is genetically
8transmitted from parent to offspring and may cause illness or disease.
AB949,4,139(d) “Orthopedic condition” means a condition affecting bones, skeletal muscle,
10cartilage, tendons, ligaments, or joints, or any combination thereof, including elbow

12cranial cruciate ligaments. “Orthopedic condition” does not include cancer or a
13metabolic, hemopoietic, or autoimmune disease.
AB949,4,1514(e) “Pet insurance” means a property insurance policy issued in this state that

18being of a pet and that is separate from a pet insurance policy, is not included on a
19pet insurance policy form, and does not shift the risk of loss in exchange for
20premiums.

23under, a pet insurance policy:
