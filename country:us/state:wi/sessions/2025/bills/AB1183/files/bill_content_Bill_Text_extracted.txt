Referred to Committee on Education.AB1183,1,31An Act to create 20.255 (2) (bj) and 115.374 of the statutes; relating to: grants 2for emergency notification systems that are accessible to deaf and hard-of-3hearing pupils and making an appropriation.Analysis by the Legislative Reference BureauUnder this bill, the Department of Public Instruction must establish a grant program to award grants to school districts that install emergency notification systems that are accessible to deaf and hard-of-hearing pupils or upgrade emergency notification systems to make them accessible to deaf and hard-of-hearing pupils. Each grant to a school district may not exceed the amount the school district expended in installing or upgrading an emergency notification system. The bill provides $1,000,000 to DPI in each of fiscal years 2025-26 and 2026-27 for the grants.For further information see the state and local fiscal estimate, which will be printed as an appendix to this bill.The people of the state of Wisconsin, represented in senate and assembly, do enact as follows:AB1183,11Section 1. 20.005 (3) (schedule) of the statutes: at the appropriate place, 2insert the following amounts for the purposes indicated: - See PDF for table AB1183,28Section 2. 20.255 (2) (bj) of the statutes is created to read:AB1183,2,11920.255 (2) (bj) Emergency notification systems grants.
