Referred to Committee on Universities and Technical Colleges.SB1069,1,31An Act to create 20.192 (1) (d) and 238.147 of the statutes; relating to: 2creating an innovation grant program for institutions of higher education and 3making an appropriation.Analysis by the Legislative Reference BureauThis bill requires the Wisconsin Economic Development Corporation to establish and administer an Innovation Grant Program to provide to private nonprofit colleges funding for the development of new academic programs and community partnerships that are designed to meet workforce needs and infrastructure projects that benefit the broader public and that are designed to meet workforce needs. Under the bill, WEDC awards grants to these colleges to support the launch of innovative academic programs that are designed to meet workforce needs and infrastructure projects that benefit the larger public and that are designed to meet workforce needs, subject to the following limitations: 1) the grant recipient must provide matching funding; 2) the grant recipient must demonstrate a pathway for the academic program or infrastructure project to become self-sustaining; and 3) no more than 10 percent of the total amount of grants may be awarded to a single private nonprofit college.For further information see the state and local fiscal estimate, which will be printed as an appendix to this bill.The people of the state of Wisconsin, represented in senate and assembly, do enact as follows:SB1069,11Section 1. 20.005 (3) (schedule) of the statutes: at the appropriate place, 2insert the following amounts for the purposes indicated: - See PDF for table SB1069,28Section 2. 20.192 (1) (d) of the statutes is created to read:SB1069,2,10920.192 (1) (d) Innovation grant program.
