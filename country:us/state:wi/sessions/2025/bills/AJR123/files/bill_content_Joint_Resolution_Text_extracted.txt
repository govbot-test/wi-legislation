Referred to Committee on Rules.AJR123,1,21Relating to: proclaiming November 20 in 2025 and 2026 as Wisconsin’s 2Transgender Day of Remembrance.AJR123,1,63Whereas, Transgender Day of Remembrance, which occurs annually on 4November 20, is a day to memorialize those who have been murdered as a result of 5transphobia and to bring attention to the continued violence endured by the 6transgender and nonbinary community; andAJR123,1,97Whereas, Transgender Day of Remembrance was founded in 1999 by 8Gwendolyn Ann Smith, a transgender woman, to memorialize the murder of 9transgender woman Rita Hester in Allston, Massachusetts; andAJR123,1,1110Whereas, since its inception, Transgender Day of Remembrance has evolved 11into an international day of action; andAJR123,1,1312Whereas, the American Medical Association recognizes the violence that 13transgender and nonbinary people face in our society as an epidemic; andAJR123,2,214Whereas, transgender Americans face discrimination and violence at 1unacceptable rates, and in 2023, the Human Rights Campaign received reports of at 2least 33 fatal incidents against transgender and gender-expansive people; andAJR123,2,43Whereas, in 2024, at least 30 transgender or gender-expansive people were 4reported killed; andAJR123,2,65Whereas, the American value of self-determination is under attack when a 6transgender person is murdered in a crime of hate; now, therefore, be itAJR123,2,97Resolved by the assembly, the senate concurring, That the Wisconsin 8Legislature does hereby recognize and proclaim November 20 in 2025 and 2026 to 9be Wisconsin’s Transgender Day of Remembrance.AJR123,2,1010(end) Down Down /2025/related/proposals/ajr123 true proposaltext /2025/related/proposals/ajr123 proposaltext/2025/REG/AJR123 proposaltext/2025/REG/AJR123 section true Menu»2025»Related Documents»Proposal Text»AJR123: Joint Resolution Text × Details for PDF view Link (Permanent link) Bookmark this location View toggle Go to top of document Search in this chapter Search in this section Search in this agency Search in this chapter group Search in this chapter Search in this section Cross references for section Acts affecting this section References to this 1970 Statutes Annotations Appellate Court Citations Administrative Code Index Reference lines Clear highlighting
