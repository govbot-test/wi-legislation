Referred to Committee on Agriculture and Revenue.SB971,1,21An Act to create 101.14 (5) of the statutes; relating to: exempting certain 2agricultural warehouses from automatic fire sprinkler requirements.Analysis by the Legislative Reference BureauThis bill exempts specified agricultural warehouses used to store any of the following from being required to have an automatic fire sprinkler system installed: 1) liquid bulk fertilizer or liquid bulk pesticide if the Department of Agriculture, Trade and Consumer Protection requires the storage container of the liquid bulk fertilizer or pesticide to be located within a secondary containment structure that complies with rules promulgated by DATCP and 2) dry bulk fertilizer and dry bulk pesticide. The bill defines a “specified agricultural warehouse” as a building designed for the primary purpose of storing agricultural fertilizers, pesticides, fungicides, rodenticides, insecticides, and other crop protection products for distribution to agricultural producers, and the bill applies only to specified agricultural warehouses for which construction is initiated after the bill takes effect. Under the bill, the exemption applies only if the owner of a specified agricultural warehouse notifies the chief of the local fire department that water should not be used to suppress any fire at the warehouse and of any changes to the chemicals stored at the warehouse. Also, the exemption does not apply to an office or store within or attached to the specified agricultural warehouse. Under current law, the Department of Safety and Professional Services may promulgate rules requiring owners of places of employment and public buildings to install fire detection, prevention, or suppression devices.The people of the state of Wisconsin, represented in senate and assembly, do enact as follows:SB971,11Section 1. 101.14 (5) of the statutes is created to read:SB971,2,22101.14 (5) (a) In this subsection:SB971,2,331. “Automatic fire sprinkler system” has the meaning given in s. 145.01 (2).SB971,2,742. “Specified agricultural warehouse” means a building designed for the 5primary purpose of storing agricultural fertilizers, pesticides, fungicides, 6rodenticides, insecticides, and other crop protection products for distribution to 7agricultural producers.SB971,2,138(b) Notwithstanding subs.
