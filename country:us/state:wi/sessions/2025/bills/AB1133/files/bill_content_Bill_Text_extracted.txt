Referred to Committee on Housing and Real Estate.AB1133,1,21An Act to create 66.0104 (1) (am) and 66.0104 (4) of the statutes; relating to: 2local regulation of landlords and tenants.Analysis by the Legislative Reference BureauCurrent law prohibits cities, villages, towns, and counties (local governments) from enacting ordinances that impose certain limitations on residential landlords, including 1) restricting a landlord in entering into a rental agreement with a prospective tenant while the premises is occupied by a current tenant; 2) restricting a landlord in showing a premises to a prospective tenant during a current tenant’s tenancy; or 3) requiring a landlord to provide to a tenant information that is not required to be provided under federal or state law.This bill creates an exception to these restrictions, allowing a local government to enact an ordinance that 1) requires a landlord to make a good faith offer to renew a tenant’s lease unless there is good cause for nonrenewal, and establishes standards for determining good cause; 2) prescribes notice requirements related to a landlord’s offer to renew a lease; 3) establishes requirements related to the execution of a renewed lease agreement; 4) prescribes timeframes during which a landlord may enter a leased premises to show the premises to prospective tenants or lease the premises to another tenant for a subsequent lease period; or 5) provides a penalty or remedy for violation of the ordinance.The people of the state of Wisconsin, represented in senate and assembly, do enact as follows:AB1133,11Section 1. 66.0104 (1) (am) of the statutes is created to read:AB1133,2,2266.0104 (1) (am) “Lease” has the meaning given in s. 704.01 (1).AB1133,23Section 2. 66.0104 (4) of the statutes is created to read:AB1133,2,6466.0104 (4) Notwithstanding sub.
