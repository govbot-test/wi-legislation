Referred to Committee on Natural Resources, Veteran and Military Affairs.SB983,1,31An Act to amend 45.51 (2) (a) 1.; to create 45.01 (12) (jm) of the statutes; 2relating to: the definition of “veteran” for purposes of certain state veterans 3benefits.Analysis by the Legislative Reference BureauThis bill expands the definition of “veteran” under state law to include any current or former member of the national guard who met the requirements for satisfactory completion of his or her initial term of obligated service and received an honorable discharge or a general discharge under honorable conditions. The bill extends most veterans benefits to anyone who meets this expanded definition of veteran; however, admission to a state veterans home and burial in a veterans cemetery are not included benefits as those benefits are subject to federal regulation.The people of the state of Wisconsin, represented in senate and assembly, do enact as follows:SB983,14Section 1. 45.01 (12) (jm) of the statutes is created to read:SB983,2,3545.01 (12) (jm) A current or former member of the national guard who met the 1requirements for satisfactory completion of his or her initial term of obligated 2service and received an honorable discharge or a general discharge under honorable 3conditions.SB983,24Section 2. 45.51 (2) (a) 1. of the statutes is amended to read:SB983,2,6545.51 (2) (a) 1.
