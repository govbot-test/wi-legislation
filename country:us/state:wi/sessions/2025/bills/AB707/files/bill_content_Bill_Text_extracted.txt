Referred to Committee on State Affairs.AB707,1,21An Act to amend 20.370 (2) (cr), 77.88 (2) (ac) 1. and 77.88 (5m) of the statutes; 2relating to: managed forest land recording fees.Analysis by the Legislative Reference BureauUnder current law, the Department of Natural Resources administers the managed forest land (MFL) program, under which the owner of a parcel of land designated as MFL makes an annual acreage share payment in lieu of property taxes. In exchange, the owner must comply with certain forestry practices and, subject to exceptions, must open the land to the public for recreational activities. Certain actions relating to a parcel designated as MFL must be recorded by the appropriate register of deeds, and DNR must pay any required fee for the recording.Under current law, MFL may be transferred from one owner to another with payment of a $100 fee, which is deposited into the conservation fund. Of that amount, $30 is credited to a DNR appropriation for the payment of registers of deeds fees. Land may also be withdrawn from the MFL program with payment of a $300 fee.Under this bill, $100 transfer fees and $300 withdrawal fees are deposited into the conservation fund and credited to the DNR appropriation for the payment of fees to the registers of deeds.For further information see the state and local fiscal estimate, which will be printed as an appendix to this bill.The people of the state of Wisconsin, represented in senate and assembly, do enact as follows:AB707,11Section 1. 20.370 (2) (cr) of the statutes is amended to read:AB707,2,5220.370 (2) (cr) Forestry — recording fees. From the conservation fund, all 3moneys received under ss. 77.82 (2m) (d) and (4) and 77.88 (1) (c), (2) (ac) 1., 2., and 43., (am), and (c), (3), (3j) (c), and (3m) for the payment of fees to the registers of 5deeds under s. 77.91 (5).AB707,26Section 2. 77.88 (2) (ac) 1. of the statutes is amended to read:AB707,2,21777.88 (2) (ac) 1. If the land transferred under par. (a) meets the eligibility 8requirements under s. 77.82 (1) (a), (ag), and (b), the land shall continue to be 9designated as managed forest land if the transferee, within 30 days after a transfer 10of ownership, files a form provided by the department signed by the transferee. By 11signing the form, the transferee certifies to the department an intent to comply 12with the existing management plan for the land and any amendments to the plan. 13The transferee shall provide proof that each person holding any encumbrance on 14the land agrees to the designation. The transferee may designate an area of the 15transferred land closed to public access as provided under s. 77.83. The department 16shall issue an order continuing the designation of the land as managed forest land 17under the new ownership. The transferee shall pay a $100 fee that will accompany 18the report. The fee shall be deposited in the conservation fund. Twenty dollars of 19the fee or a different amount of the fee as may be established under subd. 2. shall be 20and credited to the appropriation under s. 20.370 (2) (cr). The department shall 21immediately notify each person entitled to notice under s. 77.82 (8).AB707,322Section 3. 77.88 (5m) of the statutes is amended to read:AB707,3,4177.88 (5m) Withdrawal fee. The withdrawal fee assessed by the 2department under subs. (1) (c), (2) (ac) 2. and 3., (am), and (c), (3), and (3j) (c), and 3(3m) shall be $300. The fee shall be deposited in the conservation fund and credited 4to the appropriation under s. 20.370 (2) (cr).AB707,3,55(end) Down Down /2025/related/proposals/ab707 true proposaltext /2025/related/proposals/ab707 proposaltext/2025/REG/AB707 proposaltext/2025/REG/AB707 section true Menu»2025»Related Documents»Proposal Text»AB707: Bill Text × Details for PDF view Link (Permanent link) Bookmark this location View toggle Go to top of document Search in this chapter Search in this section Search in this agency Search in this chapter group Search in this chapter Search in this section Cross references for section Acts affecting this section References to this 1970 Statutes Annotations Appellate Court Citations Administrative Code Index Reference lines Clear highlighting
