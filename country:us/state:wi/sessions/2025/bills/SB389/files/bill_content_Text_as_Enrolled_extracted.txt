(intro.) of the statutes; relating to: the per pupil adjustment for school district revenue limits.The people of the state of Wisconsin, represented in senate and assembly, do enact as follows:SB389,1Section 1. 121.905 (3) (c) 6. of the statutes is amended to read:121.905 (3) (c) 6. For the limit for each of the 2015-16 to 2018-19 school years, for the 2021-22 school year, for the 2027-28 school year, and for any school year thereafter, make no adjustment to the result under par. (b).SB389,2Section 2. 121.905 (3) (c) 9. of the statutes is amended to read:121.905 (3) (c) 9. For the limit for 2023-2425, add Add $325 to the result under par. (b) for the limit for the 2023-24 to 2026-2027 school years.SB389,3Section 3. 121.91 (2m) (j) (intro.) of the statutes is amended to read:121.91 (2m) (j) (intro.) Notwithstanding par. (i) and except as provided in subs. (3), (4), and (8), a school district cannot increase its revenues for each school year beginning with the 2020-21 school year-year 2425 year and ending with the 2026-27 school year to an amount that exceeds the amount calculated as follows:SB389,4Section 4. 121.91 (2m) (t) 1. (intro.) of the statutes is amended to read:121.91 (2m) (t) 1. (intro.) If 2 or more school districts are consolidated under s. 117.08 or 117.09, in the 2019-20 school year, the consolidated school district’s revenue limit shall be determined as provided under par. (im), in the 2020-21 school year, 2023-year 2425 to the 2026-27 school year, the consolidated school district’s revenue limit shall be determined as provided under par. (j), and in the 2027-28 school year and each school year thereafter, the consolidated school district’s revenue limit shall be determined as provided under par. (i), except as follows:SB389,5Section 5. Effective date.(1) This act takes effect on January 1, 2027.
