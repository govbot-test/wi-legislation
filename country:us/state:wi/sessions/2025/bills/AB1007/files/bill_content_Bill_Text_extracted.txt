Referred to Committee on Veterans and Military Affairs.AB1007,1,21An Act to amend 175.51 (1v) (a) of the statutes; relating to: administration of 2the veterans at risk alert system.Analysis by the Legislative Reference BureauThe Department of Justice currently administers several alert systems, under which DOJ works with law enforcement agencies, broadcasters, and others to disseminate information regarding certain cases involving endangered children, missing persons at risk, and veterans at risk. Under current law, to qualify as a “veteran at risk,” the missing person must be a veteran or an active duty member who has a physical or mental health condition that is related to his or her service. This bill removes the condition that the physical or mental health condition be related to his or her service.The people of the state of Wisconsin, represented in senate and assembly, do enact as follows:AB1007,13Section 1. 175.51 (1v) (a) of the statutes is amended to read:AB1007,2,34175.51 (1v) (a) In this subsection, “veteran at risk” means a veteran or an 5active-duty member of the armed forces, the national guard, or the military reserve 1forces of the United States who is known, based on the information provided by the 2person making the report, to have a physical or mental health condition that is 3related to his or her service.AB1007,2,44(end) Down Down /2025/related/proposals/ab1007 true proposaltext /2025/related/proposals/ab1007 proposaltext/2025/REG/AB1007 proposaltext/2025/REG/AB1007 section true Menu»2025»Related Documents»Proposal Text»AB1007: Bill Text × Details for PDF view Link (Permanent link) Bookmark this location View toggle Go to top of document Search in this chapter Search in this section Search in this agency Search in this chapter group Search in this chapter Search in this section Cross references for section Acts affecting this section References to this 1970 Statutes Annotations Appellate Court Citations Administrative Code Index Reference lines Clear highlighting
