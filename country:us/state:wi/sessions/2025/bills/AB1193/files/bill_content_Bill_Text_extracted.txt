This bill eliminates such crimes if the actors are adults.This bill also creates a sex workers’ bill of rights. The bill states that persons performing consensual sexual services, erotic performances, or related activities in exchange for something of value have rights granted to other workers in this state. Specifically, the rights include the right not to be subject to prosecution for engaging in sex work; the right to minimum wage and hour protections; the right to protection against discrimination, harassment, and unsafe working conditions; the right to access workers’ compensation and health benefits; and the right to privacy from surveillance.
