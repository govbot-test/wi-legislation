Referred to Committee on Environment.AB838,1,31An Act to create 20.370 (4) (kt) and 30.2024 of the statutes; relating to: 2making a grant for dredging of the Deerskin River in the town of Washington 3in Vilas County and making an appropriation.Analysis by the Legislative Reference BureauThis bill requires the Department of Natural Resources to award a grant of $1,750,000 to the town of Washington in Vilas County for a project to dredge the Deerskin River. The town may use the funds for the actual costs of permits, testing, dredging, and transportation of removed sediment if the costs are directly related to the dredging of the Deerskin River. Under the bill, if the town does not complete the dredging project by December 31, 2028, the town must return to the state the full amount of the grant. If the town completes the project and the actual costs incurred are less than the amount awarded, the town must return to the state the unexpended amount.For further information see the state and local fiscal estimate, which will be printed as an appendix to this bill.The people of the state of Wisconsin, represented in senate and assembly, do enact as follows:AB838,11Section 1. 20.005 (3) (schedule) of the statutes: at the appropriate place, 2insert the following amounts for the purposes indicated: - See PDF for table AB838,27Section 2. 20.370 (4) (kt) of the statutes is created to read:AB838,2,11820.370 (4) (kt) Deerskin River dredging grant.
