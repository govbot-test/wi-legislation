7against real property to recover the fee paid to record a declaration of ownership

AB831-ASA3,512Section 5. 70.17 (1) of the statutes is amended to read:
AB831-ASA3,2,201370.17 (1) Real Except as provided in sub. (3), real property shall be entered in
14the name of the land owner, if known to the assessor, otherwise to the occupant
15thereof if ascertainable, and otherwise without any name. The person holding the
16contract or certificate of sale of any real property contracted to be sold by the state,
17but not conveyed, shall be deemed the owner for such purpose. The undivided real
18estate of any deceased person may be entered to the heirs of such person without
19designating them by name. The real estate of an incorporated company shall be
20entered in the same manner as that of an individual.
AB831-ASA3,621Section 6. 70.17 (3) of the statutes is amended to read:
AB831-ASA3,3,242270.17 (3) Beginning with the property tax assessments as of January 1, 2024,
23manufactured and mobile homes, not otherwise exempt from taxation under s.
2466.0435 (3), buildings, improvements, and fixtures on leased lands, buildings,
1improvements, and fixtures on exempt lands, buildings, improvements, and
2fixtures on forest croplands, and buildings, improvements, and fixtures on managed
3forest lands shall be assessed as real property. If Unless exempt from taxation
4under s. 66.0435 (3), if buildings, improvements, and fixtures, but not the
5underlying land, are leased to a person other than the landowner or if the buildings,
6improvements, and fixtures are owned by a person other than the landowner, the
7assessor may create a separate tax parcel for the buildings, improvements, and
8fixtures, and if the assessor does not create a separate tax parcel, the real property
9lister shall create a separate tax parcel, and, upon recording of a declaration of
10ownership interest as provided in s. 59.43 (1c) (w), the assessor shall assess the
11buildings, improvements, and fixtures as real property to the owner of the
12buildings, improvements, and fixtures. The assessor may shall also create a tax
13parcel, as provided under s. 70.27, for buildings, improvements, and fixtures on
14exempt lands, buildings, improvements, and fixtures on forest croplands, and
15buildings, improvements, and fixtures on managed forest lands and, upon recording
16of a declaration of ownership interest, the assessor shall assess the buildings,
17improvements, and fixtures as real property to the owner of the buildings,
18improvements, and fixtures. If the owner of a property described in this subsection
19does not submit a declaration of ownership interest in the property for recording as
20provided in s. 59.43 (1c) (w), the county in which the property is located shall
21submit a declaration of ownership interest that identifies the owner of the property
22for recording as provided in s. 59.43 (1c) (w). For purposes of this subsection,
23“buildings, improvements, and fixtures” does not include any property defined in s.
2470.04.
