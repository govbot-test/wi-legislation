to create 359.43 (1c) (w), 59.43 (1c) (wc), 66.0627 (2m), 70.09 (2) (e), 70.23 (1m), 706.01 4(7r) (c) and 706.02 (1) (bm) of the statutes; relating to: recording and taxation 5of real property containing a building, improvement, or fixture and consisting 6of no land.The people of the state of Wisconsin, represented in senate and assembly, do enact as follows:AB831-ASA3,17Section 1. 59.43 (1c) (w) of the statutes is created to read:AB831-ASA3,1,9859.43 (1c) (w) Record and maintain in the index under sub. (9) declarations of 9an ownership interest in parcels that satisfy all of the following:AB831-ASA3,1,10101. The parcel contains a building, improvement, or fixture.AB831-ASA3,2,112. The parcel consists of no land.AB831-ASA3,22Section 2. 59.43 (1c) (wc) of the statutes is created to read:AB831-ASA3,2,4359.43 (1c) (wc) Record and maintain in the index under sub. (9) conveyances 4of an ownership interest in parcels that are described in par. (w).AB831-ASA3,35Section 3. 66.0627 (2m) of the statutes is created to read:AB831-ASA3,2,8666.0627 (2m) The governing body of a county may impose a special charge 7against real property to recover the fee paid to record a declaration of ownership 8interest for the property as provided in s. 70.17 (3).AB831-ASA3,49Section 4. 70.09 (2) (e) of the statutes is created to read:AB831-ASA3,2,111070.09 (2) (e) To create tax parcels for buildings, improvements, and fixtures as 11described in s. 70.17 (3).AB831-ASA3,512Section 5. 70.17 (1) of the statutes is amended to read:AB831-ASA3,2,201370.17 (1) Real Except as provided in sub. (3), real property shall be entered in 14the name of the land owner, if known to the assessor, otherwise to the occupant 15thereof if ascertainable, and otherwise without any name. The person holding the 16contract or certificate of sale of any real property contracted to be sold by the state, 17but not conveyed, shall be deemed the owner for such purpose. The undivided real 18estate of any deceased person may be entered to the heirs of such person without 19designating them by name. The real estate of an incorporated company shall be 20entered in the same manner as that of an individual.AB831-ASA3,621Section 6. 70.17 (3) of the statutes is amended to read:AB831-ASA3,3,242270.17 (3) Beginning with the property tax assessments as of January 1, 2024, 23manufactured and mobile homes, not otherwise exempt from taxation under s. 2466.0435 (3), buildings, improvements, and fixtures on leased lands, buildings, 1improvements, and fixtures on exempt lands, buildings, improvements, and 2fixtures on forest croplands, and buildings, improvements, and fixtures on managed 3forest lands shall be assessed as real property. If Unless exempt from taxation 4under s. 66.0435 (3), if buildings, improvements, and fixtures, but not the 5underlying land, are leased to a person other than the landowner or if the buildings, 6improvements, and fixtures are owned by a person other than the landowner, the 7assessor may create a separate tax parcel for the buildings, improvements, and 8fixtures, and if the assessor does not create a separate tax parcel, the real property 9lister shall create a separate tax parcel, and, upon recording of a declaration of 10ownership interest as provided in s. 59.43 (1c) (w), the assessor shall assess the 11buildings, improvements, and fixtures as real property to the owner of the 12buildings, improvements, and fixtures. The assessor may shall also create a tax 13parcel, as provided under s. 70.27, for buildings, improvements, and fixtures on 14exempt lands, buildings, improvements, and fixtures on forest croplands, and 15buildings, improvements, and fixtures on managed forest lands and, upon recording 16of a declaration of ownership interest, the assessor shall assess the buildings, 17improvements, and fixtures as real property to the owner of the buildings, 18improvements, and fixtures. If the owner of a property described in this subsection 19does not submit a declaration of ownership interest in the property for recording as 20provided in s. 59.43 (1c) (w), the county in which the property is located shall 21submit a declaration of ownership interest that identifies the owner of the property 22for recording as provided in s. 59.43 (1c) (w). For purposes of this subsection, 23“buildings, improvements, and fixtures” does not include any property defined in s. 2470.04.AB831-ASA3,71Section 7. 70.23 (1m) of the statutes is created to read:AB831-ASA3,4,3270.23 (1m) Entries required under sub. (1) shall be made based on the latest 3records of the office of the register of deeds.AB831-ASA3,84Section 8. 74.57 (4) (b) 1. of the statutes is amended to read:AB831-ASA3,4,7574.57 (4) (b) 1. Contain a legal description of each parcel of property. A legal 6description that satisfies the requirements under s. 706.01 is a sufficient 7description for purposes of this subdivision.AB831-ASA3,98Section 9. 75.12 (2) of the statutes is amended to read:AB831-ASA3,4,16975.12 (2) Such notice shall state the date of the tax certificate, the description 10of the lands involved, the amount of the delinquency and that such amount will bear 11interest and penalty as provided by law, and shall give notice that after the 12expiration of 3 months from the date of service of such notice the county will apply 13for a tax deed. A description that satisfies the requirements under s. 706.01 is a 14sufficient description for purposes of this subsection. A notice of application for a 15tax deed shall not be served earlier than 88 days prior to the earliest date on which 16the county is entitled to a deed.AB831-ASA3,1017Section 10. 75.521 (3) (am) 1. of the statutes is amended to read:AB831-ASA3,4,241875.521 (3) (am) 1. A brief description sufficient to identify each parcel affected 19by such tax lien. The description shall state the lot, and block and section number, 20if assigned, of any parcel upon any tract, the plat or map of which is recorded and 21filed in the office of the register of deeds of such county. If the lands be unplatted an 22engineer’s metes and bounds description shall be a sufficient description. A 23description that satisfies the requirements under s. 706.01 is a sufficient 24description for purposes of this subdivision.AB831-ASA3,111Section 11. 77.21 (1m) of the statutes is amended to read:AB831-ASA3,5,4277.21 (1m) “Real estate” includes, but is not limited to, buildings, 3improvements, and fixtures; roots, vines and trees of perennial crops; stock in a 4cooperative building; improvements on leased land; timber; and minerals.AB831-ASA3,125Section 12. 706.001 (1) of the statutes is amended to read:AB831-ASA3,5,96706.001 (1) Subject to the exclusions in sub. (2), this chapter shall govern 7every transaction by which any interest in land or real property described in s. 870.17 (3), is created, aliened, mortgaged, assigned or may be otherwise affected in 9law or in equity.AB831-ASA3,1310Section 13. 706.01 (7r) (c) of the statutes is created to read:AB831-ASA3,5,1411706.01 (7r) (c) For a parcel that contains a building, improvement, or fixture 12and that consists of no land, by a description of the building, improvement, or 13fixture on a form prescribed by the department of revenue and a legal description of 14the land on which the building, improvement, or fixture is located.AB831-ASA3,1415Section 14. 706.02 (1) (b) of the statutes is amended to read:AB831-ASA3,5,1616706.02 (1) (b) Identifies the land or real property described in s. 70.17 (3); andAB831-ASA3,1517Section 15. 706.02 (1) (bm) of the statutes is created to read:AB831-ASA3,5,2018706.02 (1) (bm) If a declaration of ownership has been recorded under s. 59.43 19(1c) (w) or a conveyance has been recorded under s. 59.43 (1c) (wc), identifies the 20building, improvement, or fixture.AB831-ASA3,1621Section 16. 706.05 (2m) (b) 3. of the statutes is amended to read:AB831-ASA3,5,2322706.05 (2m) (b) 3. Descriptions of property specified under s. 70.17 (3) or 23706.01 (7r) (c).AB831-ASA3,1724Section 17. Initial applicability.AB831-ASA3,6,31(1) Conveyances of buildings, improvements, or fixtures that consist 2of no land. The treatment of ss. 706.001 (1) and 706.02 (1) (b) and (bm) first 3applies to transactions settled on the effective date of this subsection.AB831-ASA3,6,44(end) Down Down /2025/related/amendments/ab831/asa3_ab831 true amends /2025/related/amendments/ab831/asa3_ab831 amends/2025/REG/AB831-ASA3 amends/2025/REG/AB831-ASA3 section true Menu»2025»Related Documents»Amendments»AB831»Amendment ASA3-AB831 × Details for PDF view Link (Permanent link) Bookmark this location View toggle Go to top of document Search in this chapter Search in this section Search in this agency Search in this chapter group Search in this chapter Search in this section Cross references for section Acts affecting this section References to this 1970 Statutes Annotations Appellate Court Citations Administrative Code Index Reference lines Clear highlighting
