Referred to Committee on Government Operations, Labor and Economic Development.SB788,1,41An Act to amend 15.165 (title), 16.50 (3) (b), 16.505 (1) (intro.), 20.923 (7) 2(intro.) and 20.923 (7) (a); to create 15.165 (5), 16.505 (2d), 40.03 (1) (dm), 340.03 (4m) and 230.08 (2) (pb) of the statutes; relating to: creating an Office 4of Internal Audit attached to the Department of Employee Trust Funds.Analysis by the Legislative Reference BureauThis bill creates an Office of Internal Audit attached to the Department of Employee Trust Funds and provides for an internal auditor within that office. Under the bill, the office plans and conducts audits of activities and programs administered by ETF, among other responsibilities.The bill requires the Employee Trust Funds Board to appoint an internal auditor outside the classified service who reports directly to the board. Currently, the internal auditor for ETF reports to the secretary of ETF. Under the bill, the internal auditor is assigned to general senior executive group 1, which is the same group to which the assistant deputy secretary of ETF is assigned.For further information see the state fiscal estimate, which will be printed as an appendix to this bill.The people of the state of Wisconsin, represented in senate and assembly, do enact as follows:SB788,11Section 1. 15.165 (title) of the statutes is amended to read:SB788,2,2215.165 (title) Same; attached boards and offices.SB788,23Section 2. 15.165 (5) of the statutes is created to read:SB788,2,9415.165 (5) Office of internal audit. There is created an office of internal 5audit that is attached to the department of employee trust funds under s. 15.03. 6The office shall be under the direction and supervision of an internal auditor who 7shall be appointed by the employee trust funds board and serve outside the 8classified service. The internal auditor shall report directly to the employee trust 9funds board.SB788,310Section 3. 16.50 (3) (b) of the statutes is amended to read:SB788,2,171116.50 (3) (b) No change in the number of full-time equivalent positions 12authorized through the biennial budget process or other legislative act may be made 13without the approval of the joint committee on finance, except for position changes 14made by the governor under s. 16.505 (1) (c), (2), or (2j), by the investment board 15under s. 16.505 (2g), by the employee trust funds board under s. 16.505 (2d), or by 16the board of regents of the University of Wisconsin System under s. 16.505 (2m) or 17(2p).SB788,418Section 4. 16.505 (1) (intro.) of the statutes is amended to read:SB788,2,211916.505 (1) (intro.) (intro.) The salary ranges 6for the director and the executive assistant of the Wisconsin Technical College 7System and for the secretary, deputy secretary, and assistant deputy secretary, and 8internal auditor of the department of employee trust funds shall be contained in the 9recommendations of the administrator of the division of personnel management in 10the department of administration under s. 230.12 (3) (e). The board of the 11Wisconsin Technical College System shall set the salaries for the director and 12executive assistant of the Wisconsin Technical College System within the range to 13which the positions are assigned to recognize merit, to permit orderly salary 14progression, and to recognize competitive factors.
