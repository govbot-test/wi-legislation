Referred to Committee on Rules.AJR144,1,21Relating to: congratulating the U.S. Olympic Women’s Ice Hockey Team on 2winning the Olympic gold medal.AJR144,1,43Whereas, the U.S. women’s ice hockey team won 2–1 against Canada in 4overtime; andAJR144,1,85Whereas, one-quarter of the team has played for the University of 6Wisconsin–Madison women’s hockey team, featuring current Badgers Caroline 7“KK” Harvey, Kirsten Simms, Ava McNaughton, and Laila Edwards, alongside 8alumnae Britta Curl-Salemme and Hilary Knight; andAJR144,1,109Whereas, former Badger Hilary Knight tipped in a game-tying goal on a shot 10by current Badger Laila Edwards to force overtime; andAJR144,1,1311Whereas, five-time Olympic medalist Hilary Knight, who has three silver 12medals (2010, 2014, and 2022) and now two gold medals (2018 and 2026), made 13U.S. history with her record-setting 15th career goal; andAJR144,2,21Whereas, Megan Keller took a long stretch pass, dangled past a Canadian 2defender, and scored the winning goal that secured the gold medal for the team; andAJR144,2,43Whereas, Laila Edwards became the first Black hockey player to win a gold 4medal for the United States; andAJR144,2,65Whereas, Caroline Harvey co-led the tournament in scoring, set a record for 6points by a defender, and won MVP of the tournament; and AJR144,2,127Whereas, the team was led by captain Hilary Knight and alternate captains 8Alex Carpenter and Megan Keller, who were joined by teammates Hannah Bilka, 9Britta Curl-Salemme, Kendall Coyne Schofield, Joy Dunne, Taylor Heise, Tessa 10Janecke, Abbey Murphy, Kelly Pannek, Hayley Scamurra, Kirsten Simms, Grace 11Zumwinkle, Cayla Barnes, Laila Edwards, Rory Guilday, Caroline Harvey, Lee 12Stecklein, Haley Winn, Aerin Frankel, Ava McNaughton, and Gwyneth Philips; andAJR144,2,1613Whereas, the team was managed by an experienced and dedicated coaching 14staff, including head coach John Wroblewski, native to Neenah, assistant coaches 15Shari Dickerman, Brent Hill, and Josh Sciba, and goaltending coach Alli Altmann; 16andAJR144,2,1917Whereas, the team was also assisted by fellow Wisconsinites and Badgers 18Stefanie Arndt, an athletic trainer from Delafield, and equipment manager Sis 19Paulsen from Eau Claire; andAJR144,2,2120Whereas, Wisconsin takes great pride in our Badger representation and our 21country’s strong showing at the 2026 Winter Olympics;
