Referred to Committee on Universities and Technical Colleges.SB1031,1,31An Act to create 20.235 (1) (dm) and 39.55 of the statutes; relating to: 2creating an undergraduate assistance grant program for students enrolled in 3private nonprofit postsecondary institutions and making an appropriation.Analysis by the Legislative Reference BureauThis bill creates an undergraduate assistance grant program administered by the Higher Educational Aids Board. Under the bill, a student is eligible for a grant under the program if the student 1) is enrolled in an undergraduate program at a private nonprofit postsecondary institution headquartered in this state; 2) is a resident of this state; 3) has completed the federal Free Application for Federal Student Aid and does not qualify for a federal Pell grant; and 4) has a household income of less than $60,000. The bill provides $2,500,000 annually in funding for HEAB to award grants to eligible students for up to 10 semesters.For further information see the state fiscal estimate, which will be printed as an appendix to this bill.The people of the state of Wisconsin, represented in senate and assembly, do enact as follows:SB1031,11Section 1. 20.005 (3) (schedule) of the statutes: at the appropriate place, 2insert the following amounts for the purposes indicated: - See PDF for table SB1031,28Section 2. 20.235 (1) (dm) of the statutes is created to read:SB1031,2,10920.235 (1) (dm) Undergraduate assistance grant program. (1) In this section, 13“institution of higher education” means a private nonprofit postsecondary 14institution that is headquartered in this state and that is accredited, as defined in s. 1539.30 (1) (d).SB1031,2,1716(2) The board shall establish and administer an undergraduate assistance 17grant program to provide financial assistance to eligible undergraduate students.SB1031,2,1918(3) (a) Subject to par.
