andAR11,1,96Whereas, blood donations are a backbone of Wisconsin’s health care; every two 7seconds, someone needs a blood transfusion, but blood cannot be manufactured and 8is only made possible by generous donors who roll up their sleeves to help save lives; 9andAR11,1,1310Whereas, the Wisconsin population is aging, and too few new donors are 11volunteering to take the place of those unable to donate any longer; only about 3 12percent of the age-eligible population donates blood, but patient needs are constant;
