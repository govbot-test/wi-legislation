Referred to Committee on Rules.AJR119,1,21Relating to: recognizing and congratulating the Stevens Point Area Senior High 2boys cross country team.AJR119,1,63Whereas, on November 1, 2025, the Stevens Point Area Senior High (SPASH) 4boys cross country team continued its tradition of excellence by winning the 5Wisconsin Interscholastic Athletic Association (WIAA) State Cross Country 6Tournament; andAJR119,1,97Whereas, the SPASH boys cross country team has represented the Stevens 8Point community with pride, discipline, and an unwavering commitment to athletic 9and academic achievement; andAJR119,1,1210Whereas, the program fields an extraordinary 70 high school athletes and 40 11junior high athletes, fostering a culture of teamwork, leadership, and long-term 12personal development; andAJR119,2,213Whereas, during the 2025 season, the Panthers completed an undefeated 14campaign, capturing titles at seven multi-team invitationals, the Wisconsin Valley 1Conference Meet, the WIAA sectional meet, and, ultimately, the WIAA state 2championship; andAJR119,2,63Whereas, this state championship marks the fourth consecutive title for the 4SPASH boys cross country team, its fifth in the past six years and an astounding 515th overall, further cementing the program’s standing as one of the most 6accomplished in Wisconsin history; andAJR119,2,107Whereas, between 1977 and 2024, the SPASH boys cross country team 8qualified for the WIAA state meet an unprecedented 47 times—the most state 9appearances of any team in Wisconsin, demonstrating unmatched consistency and 10excellence across generations of student-athletes; andAJR119,2,1311Whereas, the team’s success extended beyond competition as they were 12honored as Academic All-State, exemplifying the balance of hard work in the 13classroom and on the course; andAJR119,2,1614Whereas, head coaches Donn Behnke and Kevin Hopp were recognized as 15State Coaches of the Year for their exceptional leadership and dedication to 16developing student-athletes in mind, body, and character; andAJR119,2,1917Whereas, the Panthers also celebrated outstanding individual achievements, 18including First Team All-Conference honors for Cooper Erickson, Garrett Strong, 19Fisher Carroll, John Jazdzewski, and Bennett Story;
