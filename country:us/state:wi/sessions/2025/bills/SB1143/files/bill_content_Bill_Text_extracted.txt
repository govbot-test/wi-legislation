Referred to Committee on Education.SB1143,1,31An Act to create 20.255 (2) (bk) and 115.375 of the statutes; relating to: 2grants for school district construction of or upgrades to accessible playgrounds 3and making an appropriation.Analysis by the Legislative Reference BureauUnder this bill, the Department of Public Instruction must establish a grant program to award grants to school districts that construct playgrounds that are accessible to pupils with disabilities or upgrade playgrounds to make them accessible to pupils with disabilities. Each grant to a school district may not exceed the amount the school district expended in constructing or upgrading a playground. The bill provides $1,000,000 to DPI in each of fiscal years 2025-26 and 2026-27 for the grants.For further information see the state and local fiscal estimate, which will be printed as an appendix to this bill.The people of the state of Wisconsin, represented in senate and assembly, do enact as follows:SB1143,14Section 1. 20.005 (3) (schedule) of the statutes: at the appropriate place, 5insert the following amounts for the purposes indicated: - See PDF for table SB1143,25Section 2. 20.255 (2) (bk) of the statutes is created to read:SB1143,2,7620.255 (2) (bk) Grants for accessible playgrounds. The amounts in the 7schedule for the grants for accessible playgrounds under s. 115.375.SB1143,38Section 3. 115.375 of the statutes is created to read:SB1143,2,159115.375 Grants for accessible playgrounds. From the appropriation 10under s. 20.255 (2) (bk), the department shall establish a grant program to award 11grants to school districts that construct a playground that is accessible to pupils 12with disabilities or upgrade a playground to make it accessible to pupils with 13disabilities.
