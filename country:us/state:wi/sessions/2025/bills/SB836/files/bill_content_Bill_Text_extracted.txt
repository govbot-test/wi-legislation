Referred to Committee on Education.SB836,1,21An Act to amend 118.07 (4) (bm) 1.; to create 115.28 (44) of the statutes; 2relating to: school lockdown announcements.Analysis by the Legislative Reference BureauThis bill requires the Department of Public Instruction, in consultation with the Office of School Safety in the Department of Justice, to create a prerecorded audio message to announce a school lockdown. Additionally, DPI must make the audio message available to public, private, and tribal schools at no cost.Under current law, school boards and governing bodies of private schools must have a school safety plan that includes an individualized safety plan for each school building and facility that is regularly occupied by pupils. The bill requires that each individualized safety plan incorporate the school lockdown audio message created by DPI to announce a school lockdown required in the plan.For further information see the state fiscal estimate, which will be printed as an appendix to this bill.The people of the state of Wisconsin, represented in senate and assembly, do enact as follows:SB836,13Section 1. 115.28 (44) of the statutes is created to read:SB836,2,34115.28 (44) School safety; lockdown announcement. In consultation 1with the office of school safety in the department of justice, create and make 2available to public, private, and tribal schools at no cost, a prerecorded audio 3message to announce a school lockdown.SB836,24Section 2. 118.07 (4) (bm) 1. of the statutes is amended to read:SB836,2,105118.07 (4) (bm) 1. An individualized safety plan for each school building and 6facility that is regularly occupied by pupils. The individualized safety plan shall 7include any real property related to the school building or facility that is regularly 8occupied by pupils. If an individualized safety plan includes a school lockdown, the 9plan shall incorporate the audio message created under s. 115.28 (44) to announce 10any school lockdown required under the plan.SB836,2,1111(end) Down Down /2025/related/proposals/sb836 true proposaltext /2025/related/proposals/sb836 proposaltext/2025/REG/SB836 proposaltext/2025/REG/SB836 section true Menu»2025»Related Documents»Proposal Text»SB836: Bill Text × Details for PDF view Link (Permanent link) Bookmark this location View toggle Go to top of document Search in this chapter Search in this section Search in this agency Search in this chapter group Search in this chapter Search in this section Cross references for section Acts affecting this section References to this 1970 Statutes Annotations Appellate Court Citations Administrative Code Index Reference lines Clear highlighting
