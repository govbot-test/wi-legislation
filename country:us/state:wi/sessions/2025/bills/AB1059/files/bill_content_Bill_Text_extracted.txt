Referred to Committee on Health, Aging and Long-Term Care.AB1059,1,21An Act relating to: funding for home-delivered meals and making an 2appropriation.Analysis by the Legislative Reference BureauUnder current law, the Department of Health Services and the federal government provide funding for home-delivered meals for seniors.
