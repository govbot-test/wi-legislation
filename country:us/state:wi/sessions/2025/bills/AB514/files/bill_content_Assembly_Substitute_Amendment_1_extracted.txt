This bill requires the director of state courts to add four circuit court branches: one in Menominee and Shawano Counties beginning August 1, 2028, one in Brown County beginning August 1, 2028, and one in Kenosha County beginning August 1, 2028, and one in Brown County beginning August 1, 2029. The bill provides that in order to receive those additional circuit court branches, the counties must pass resolutions requesting the additional branches, and if the counties fail to do so the director of state courts may reallocate those circuit court branches to another county that has passed a resolution requesting an additional circuit court branch.This bill provides position authority for many court and criminal justice system employees in the 2027-29 and 2029-31 bienniums, as follows:1. Beginning August 1, 2028, three judges.2. Beginning August 1, 2029, one judge.3. Beginning August 1, 2028, three court reporters.4. Beginning August 1, 2029, one court reporter.5. Beginning July 1, 2027, 28.5 assistant district attorneys.6. Beginning July 1, 2028, 40 assistant district attorneys.7. Beginning July 1, 2027, 12 assistant state public defenders.8. Beginning July 1, 2028, six assistant state public defenders.9. Beginning July 1, 2027, 21 state public defender support staff positions.10. Beginning July 1, 2028, 14 state public defender support staff positions.The bill requires the director of state courts, the district attorneys, and the public defender board to include a request for funding for the positions authorized under this bill in their 2027-29 and 2029-31 biennial budget requests.This bill also requires the director of state courts, the public defender board, and the district attorneys to submit to the legislature by December 31, 2032 a report that analyzes the workload and assesses the personnel needs for their operations.The people of the state of Wisconsin, represented in senate and assembly, do enact as follows:AB514-ASA1,11Section 1. 753.0605 (4) to (7) of the statutes are created to read:AB514-ASA1,2,132753.0605 (4) By November 14, 2027, the director of state courts shall add one 3additional circuit court branch, to begin operation on August 1, 2028. If 4Menominee and Shawano Counties have passed resolutions requesting an 5additional circuit court branch for Menominee and Shawano Counties by July 31, 62027, the director of state courts shall allocate the branch under this subsection to 7the circuit court for Menominee and Shawano counties. If Menominee and 8Shawano Counties have not passed resolutions requesting an additional circuit 9court branch for Menominee and Shawano Counties by July 31, 2027, the director of 10state courts may allocate the branch under this subsection to any county that the 11director of state courts determines is in need of an additional circuit court branch 12that has passed a resolution requesting an additional circuit court branch by July 1331, 2027.AB514-ASA1,3,914(5) By November 14, 2027, the director of state courts shall add one additional 1circuit court branch, to begin operation on August 1, 2028. If Kenosha County has 2passed a resolution requesting an additional circuit court branch for Kenosha 3County by July 31, 2027, the director of state courts shall allocate the branch under 4this subsection to the circuit court for Kenosha County. If Kenosha County has not 5passed a resolution requesting an additional circuit court branch for Kenosha 6County by July 31, 2027, the director of state courts may allocate the branch under 7this subsection to any county that the director of state courts determines is in need 8of an additional circuit court branch that has passed a resolution requesting an 9additional circuit court branch by July 31, 2027.AB514-ASA1,3,1910(6) By November 14, 2027, the director of state courts shall add one additional 11circuit court branch, to begin operation on August 1, 2028. If Brown County has 12passed a resolution requesting an additional circuit court branch for Brown County 13by July 31, 2027, the director of state courts shall allocate the branch under this 14subsection to the circuit court for Brown County. If Brown County has not passed a 15resolution requesting an additional circuit court branch for Brown County by July 1631, 2027, the director of state courts may allocate the branch under this subsection 17to any county that the director of state courts determines is in need of an additional 18circuit court branch that has passed a resolution requesting an additional circuit 19court branch by July 31, 2027.AB514-ASA1,4,520(7) By November 14, 2028, the director of state courts shall add one additional 21circuit court branch, to begin operation on August 1, 2029.
