Referred to Committee on Veterans and Military Affairs.AB777,1,21An Act to create 14.16 (9r) of the statutes; relating to: Veterans Benefits 2Education Week.Analysis by the Legislative Reference BureauThis bill designates the second week of November as “Veterans Benefits Education Week,” the purpose of which is to inform veterans about the benefits to which they are entitled and assist veterans in maximizing their use of such benefits. The bill requires the governor to issue an annual proclamation for the observance of “Veterans Benefits Education Week” that highlights the benefits to which veterans are entitled.The people of the state of Wisconsin, represented in senate and assembly, do enact as follows:AB777,13Section 1. 14.16 (9r) of the statutes is created to read:AB777,2,4414.16 (9r) Veterans Benefits Education Week.
