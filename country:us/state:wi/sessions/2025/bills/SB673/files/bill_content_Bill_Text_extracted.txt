Referred to Committee on Education.SB673,1,21An Act to amend 119.04 (1); to create 120.12 (30) of the statutes; relating to: 2school board policies on appropriate communication with pupils.Analysis by the Legislative Reference BureauThis bill requires each school board to adopt, by July 1, 2026, a policy on appropriate communications between employees or volunteers of the school district and pupils enrolled in the school district, pupils attending school that are not under the control of the school board, and pupils who are homeschooled. A school board must include in the policy consequences for employees or volunteers who violate the the policy. The bill requires that the policy apply to communications that occur during or outside of school hours.
