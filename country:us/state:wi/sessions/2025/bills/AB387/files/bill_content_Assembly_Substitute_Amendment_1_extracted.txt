The bill changes from 2026 to 2025 the year specified in a statutory provision so that it is consistent with the date on which the provision takes effect.2. The bill corrects an errant cross-reference in an effective date provision to reflect changes made by Senate Amendment 26 to Senate Substitute Amendment 2 to Senate Bill 45, which was adopted.3. The bill increases the amount appropriated to the Joint Committee on Finance for supplemental appropriations by $2,600,000 for fiscal year 2025-26 to supplement the appropriation to the Department of Transportation for the purpose of the public protective services hearing protection program.4. The bill clarifies that if a University of Wisconsin System building project that was previously funded solely through gifts and grants was enumerated in Act 15, the Department of Administration may assume any existing architect or engineer contract with respect to the project.The people of the state of Wisconsin, represented in senate and assembly, do enact as follows:AB387-ASA1,11Section 1. 18.08 (7) of the statutes is amended to read:AB387-ASA1,2,8218.08 (7) Notwithstanding sub.
