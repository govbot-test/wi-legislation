Referred to Committee on Health.SB1002,1,21An Act to amend 250.20 (3) of the statutes; relating to: minority health 2grants.Analysis by the Legislative Reference BureauThis bill adds a funding priority for the minority health program through which the Department of Health Services annually awards grants to organizations for activities to improve the health status of economically disadvantaged minority group members. Under current law, DHS awards grants of up to $50,000 per year per applicant and requires that applicants that are not federally qualified health centers receive priority for grants. This bill requires that applicants providing maternal and child health services also receive priority for these grants.The people of the state of Wisconsin, represented in senate and assembly, do enact as follows:SB1002,13Section 1. 250.20 (3) of the statutes is amended to read:SB1002,2,84250.20 (3) From the appropriation account under s. 20.435 (1) (cr), the 5department shall annually award grants for activities to improve the health status 6of economically disadvantaged minority group members. A person may apply, in 1the manner specified by the department, for a grant of up to $50,000 in each fiscal 2year to conduct these activities. An awardee of a grant under this subsection shall 3provide, for at least 50 percent of the grant amount, matching funds that may 4consist of funding or an in-kind contribution. An applicant that is not a federally 5qualified health center, as defined under 42 CFR 405.2401 (b) shall receive priority 6for grants awarded under this subsection. An applicant that provides maternal and 7child health services shall receive priority for grants awarded under this 8subsection.SB1002,2,99(end) Down Down /2025/related/proposals/sb1002 true proposaltext /2025/related/proposals/sb1002 proposaltext/2025/REG/SB1002 proposaltext/2025/REG/SB1002 section true Menu»2025»Related Documents»Proposal Text»SB1002: Bill Text × Details for PDF view Link (Permanent link) Bookmark this location View toggle Go to top of document Search in this chapter Search in this section Search in this agency Search in this chapter group Search in this chapter Search in this section Cross references for section Acts affecting this section References to this 1970 Statutes Annotations Appellate Court Citations Administrative Code Index Reference lines Clear highlighting
