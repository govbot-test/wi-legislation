Referred to Committee on Energy and Utilities.AB1162,1,21An Act to create 196.03 (7) of the statutes; relating to: a very large customer 2class for electric utilities and granting rule-making authority.Analysis by the Legislative Reference BureauThis bill requires the Public Service Commission to establish by order the definition and appropriate characteristics of a very large customer class or subclass for each electric utility. The bill requires any electric utility that offers a tariff or contract rate to a very large customer to file a rate case application with PSC by April 1 of every other year. In such a rate case, the bill requires the electric utility to provide information, according to rules promulgated by PSC, necessary for PSC to determine the total costs the electric utility incurs, and is forecasted to incur, in providing service to the very large customer under the tariff or contract rate. Considering the information provided by the electric utility, the bill requires PSC to determine if the rates charged to the very large customer are just and reasonable or whether they are unreasonable, preferential, or unjustly discriminatory. If deemed unreasonable, preferential, or unjustly discriminatory, the bill requires PSC to adjust rates or modify the terms of service for the very large customer in a manner appropriate to make the rates just and reasonable.For further information see the state fiscal estimate, which will be printed as an appendix to this bill.The people of the state of Wisconsin, represented in senate and assembly, do enact as follows:AB1162,11Section 1. 196.03 (7) of the statutes is created to read:AB1162,2,22196.03 (7) (a) In this subsection:AB1162,2,331. “Electric utility” has the meaning given in s. 196.378 (1) (d).AB1162,2,542. “Very large customer” has the meaning assigned by the commission to a 5class or subclass of an electric utility’s customers by order under par. (b).AB1162,2,96(b) By December 15, 2027, the commission shall establish by order the 7definition and appropriate characteristics of a very large customer class or subclass 8for each electric utility. The commission may satisfy the requirement under this 9paragraph in a rate case for the electric utility or in another proceeding.AB1162,2,1410(c) Any electric utility that offers a tariff or contract rate to a very large 11customer shall file a rate case application with the commission by April 1 of every 12other year. The chair of the commission may issue a waiver of the rate case 13application filing deadline if the electric utility demonstrates exceptional 14circumstances that necessitate such a waiver. AB1162,3,515(d) In a rate case under par. (c), the electric utility shall provide information 16necessary for the commission to determine the total costs the electric utility incurs, 17and is forecasted to incur, in providing service to the very large customer under the 18tariff or contract rate. The commission shall promulgate rules to establish the 19specific requirements electric utilities shall follow to justify how the current or 20proposed rates in a rate case under this subsection pay for the costs of providing 21service to a very large customer. Considering the information provided by the 22electric utility, the commission shall determine whether the rates charged to the 1very large customer are just and reasonable under sub. (1) and whether they are 2unreasonable, preferential, or unjustly discriminatory under s. 196.37. If deemed 3unreasonable, preferential, or unjustly discriminatory, the commission shall adjust 4rates or modify the terms of service for the very large customer in a manner 5appropriate to bring the rates into compliance with sub. (1) and s. 196.37.AB1162,26Section 2. Initial applicability.AB1162,3,97(1) Electric service to very large customers. The treatment of s. 196.03 8(7) first applies to a tariff or agreement submitted to the commission for its review 9on the effective date of this subsection.AB1162,3,1010(end) Down Down /2025/related/proposals/ab1162 true proposaltext /2025/related/proposals/ab1162 proposaltext/2025/REG/AB1162 proposaltext/2025/REG/AB1162 section true Menu»2025»Related Documents»Proposal Text»AB1162: Bill Text × Details for PDF view Link (Permanent link) Bookmark this location View toggle Go to top of document Search in this chapter Search in this section Search in this agency Search in this chapter group Search in this chapter Search in this section Cross references for section Acts affecting this section References to this 1970 Statutes Annotations Appellate Court Citations Administrative Code Index Reference lines Clear highlighting
