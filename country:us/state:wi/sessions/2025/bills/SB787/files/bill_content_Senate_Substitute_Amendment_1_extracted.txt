This bill increases the $10,000 limit for those cases to $15,000 on January 1, 2027.Current law provides that certain consumer transactions and leases are not covered by the Wisconsin Consumer Act and, if the amount financed is $25,000 or less, these actions must be brought in small claims court. The bill increases that amount to $50,000 on January 1, 2027.The people of the state of Wisconsin, represented in senate and assembly, do enact as follows:SB787-SSA1,14Section 1. 421.202 (6) of the statutes is amended to read:SB787-SSA1,2,41421.202 (6) Consumer credit transactions in which the amount financed 2exceeds $25,000 $50,000, motor vehicle consumer leases in which the total lease 3obligation exceeds $25,000 $50,000, or other consumer transactions in which the 4cash price exceeds $25,000 $50,000.SB787-SSA1,25Section 2. 799.01 (1) (c) of the statutes is amended to read:SB787-SSA1,2,76799.01 (1) (c) Replevins.
