Referred to Committee on Utilities, Technology and Tourism.SB1092,1,21An Act to repeal 196.03 (5r); to create 196.03 (5r) of the statutes; relating to: 2a cap on rate increases for retail electric service.Analysis by the Legislative Reference BureauThis bill prohibits the Public Service Commission from authorizing rates for retail electric service that exceed an increase of 5 percent over the utility’s rates in the prior year. The bill sunsets this prohibition on January 1, 2029.The people of the state of Wisconsin, represented in senate and assembly, do enact as follows:SB1092,13Section 1. 196.03 (5r) of the statutes is created to read:SB1092,1,64196.03 (5r) The commission may not authorize rates for retail electric service 5that exceed an increase of 5 percent over rates in place for that utility in the prior 6year.SB1092,27Section 2. 196.03 (5r) of the statutes, as affected by 2025 Wisconsin Act .... 8(this act), is repealed.SB1092,31Section 3.
