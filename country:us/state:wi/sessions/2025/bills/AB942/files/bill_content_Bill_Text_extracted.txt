Referred to Committee on Campaigns and Elections.AB942,1,21An Act to amend 5.25 (3) (c) of the statutes; relating to: notice of change in 2polling place location.Analysis by the Legislative Reference BureauCurrent law requires municipalities to establish polling places for each election at least 30 days before the election. Under current law, after an election and more than 30 days before the next election, the location of a polling place in a municipality may not be discontinued without the approval of the municipality’s governing body after a public hearing at which the public has an opportunity to present testimony on the proposed discontinuation. Under this bill, if the location of a polling place is discontinued after an election, notice of that change must be mailed to each residence the polling place serves no later than 30 days before the next election.For further information see the local fiscal estimate, which will be printed as an appendix to this bill.The people of the state of Wisconsin, represented in senate and assembly, do enact as follows:AB942,13Section 1. 5.25 (3) (c) of the statutes is amended to read:AB942,2,815.25 (3) (c) After an election and more than 30 days before the next election, 2the location of a polling place in a municipality may not be discontinued without the 3approval of the municipality’s governing body after a public hearing at which the 4public has an opportunity to present testimony on the proposed discontinuation. If 5the location of a polling place is discontinued after an election, the municipal clerk 6or board of election commissioners shall, no later than 30 days before the next 7election, provide notice of that change by mail to each residence in the area served 8by the polling place.AB942,2,99(end) Down Down /2025/related/proposals/ab942 true proposaltext /2025/related/proposals/ab942 proposaltext/2025/REG/AB942 proposaltext/2025/REG/AB942 section true Menu»2025»Related Documents»Proposal Text»AB942: Bill Text × Details for PDF view Link (Permanent link) Bookmark this location View toggle Go to top of document Search in this chapter Search in this section Search in this agency Search in this chapter group Search in this chapter Search in this section Cross references for section Acts affecting this section References to this 1970 Statutes Annotations Appellate Court Citations Administrative Code Index Reference lines Clear highlighting
