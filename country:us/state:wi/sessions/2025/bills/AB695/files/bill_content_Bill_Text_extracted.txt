Referred to Committee on Housing and Real Estate.AB695,1,31An Act to create 16.265 and 20.505 (1) (ft) of the statutes; relating to: 2creating a landlord fire prevention grant program, granting rule-making 3authority, and making an appropriation.Analysis by the Legislative Reference BureauThis bill requires the Department of Administration to establish a program to award grants to landlords to assist with the costs of implementing fire prevention and suppression measures in residential buildings. Measures a grant could be awarded for include installing fire sprinklers in a building and providing fire extinguishers in residential units. The bill directs DOA to establish the application procedures for, and terms and conditions of, receiving a grant, with grants awarded on a first-come, first-served basis. Under the bill, a grant may not exceed 50 percent of the landlord’s costs for the fire prevention and suppression measures, and the total amount of grants that may be awarded under the program is $10,000,000.For further information see the state fiscal estimate, which will be printed as an appendix to this bill.The people of the state of Wisconsin, represented in senate and assembly, do enact as follows:AB695,11Section 1. 16.265 of the statutes is created to read:AB695,2,11216.265 Landlord fire prevention grant program. (1) The department 3shall establish and administer a program to award grants to landlords of 4residential buildings for the purpose of implementing fire prevention and 5suppression measures, including installing fire sprinklers in a residential building 6and providing fire extinguishers in residential units. The department shall 7establish application procedures for, and the terms and conditions of, receiving a 8grant under this subsection. The department shall award grants on a first-come, 9first-served basis to eligible landlords. A grant awarded under this subsection may 10not exceed 50 percent of the costs incurred by the landlord for the fire prevention 11and suppression measures.AB695,2,1412(2) The department shall make grants under sub.
