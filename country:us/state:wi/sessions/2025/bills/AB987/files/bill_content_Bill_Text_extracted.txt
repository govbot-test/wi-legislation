This bill strikes from this purpose statement references to overcrowding and congestion and adds references to land development and housing and transportation options.The people of the state of Wisconsin, represented in senate and assembly, do enact as follows:AB987,13Section 1. 62.23 (7) (c) of the statutes is amended to read:AB987,2,11462.23 (7) (c) Purposes in view.
