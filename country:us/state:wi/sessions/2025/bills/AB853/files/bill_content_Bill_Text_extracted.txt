Referred to Committee on Colleges and Universities.AB853,1,21An Act relating to: funding for certain research positions at the University of 2Wisconsin-Madison and making an appropriation.Analysis by the Legislative Reference BureauThis bill specifies that the Board of Regents of the University of Wisconsin System must provide funding for two agriculture and climate change research positions assigned to the Division of Extension at UW-Madison. The positions must be filled using existing, currently vacant positions but must also reflect an increase in the total number of research positions at UW-Madison.For further information see the state fiscal estimate, which will be printed as an appendix to this bill.The people of the state of Wisconsin, represented in senate and assembly, do enact as follows:AB853,13Section 1. Nonstatutory provisions.AB853,1,44(1) Climate science research positions.AB853,1,55(a) In this subsection:AB853,1,661. “Board” means the Board of Regents of the University of Wisconsin System.AB853,2,112. “Chancellor” means the chancellor of the University of Wisconsin-Madison.AB853,2,42(b) From the appropriation under s. 20.285 (1) (a), the board shall provide 3funding for 2.0 full-time equivalent research positions at the University of 4Wisconsin-Madison focusing on agriculture and climate change.AB853,2,55(c) The positions specified in par. (b) are subject to all of the following:AB853,2,761. The positions shall be filled using existing authorized full-time equivalent 7positions, funded from s. 20.285 (1) (a), that are currently vacant.AB853,2,982. The positions shall be filled in a manner that reflects an increase in the 9total number of research positions at the University of Wisconsin-Madison.AB853,2,11103. The positions shall be assigned to the division of extension at the 11University of Wisconsin-Madison.AB853,2,1312(d) The chancellor may not abolish under s. 16.505 (2p) the positions specified 13in par. (b).AB853,214Section 2. Fiscal changes.AB853,2,2215(1) UW climate science research positions. In the schedule under s. 1620.005 (3) for the appropriation to the Board of Regents of the University of 17Wisconsin System under s. 20.285 (1) (a), the dollar amount for fiscal year 2025-26 18is increased by $200,000 to provide funding for the positions specified in Section 1 19(1) of this act.
