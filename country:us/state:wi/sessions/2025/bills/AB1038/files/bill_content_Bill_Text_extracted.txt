Referred to Committee on Ways and Means.AB1038,1,21An Act to create 71.05 (6) (b) 57. of the statutes; relating to: an individual 2income tax subtraction for union dues allocated for education or training.Analysis by the Legislative Reference BureauThis bill provides an individual income tax subtraction for the amount of union dues and expenses paid by a private sector employee that are allocated for educational or training purposes.
