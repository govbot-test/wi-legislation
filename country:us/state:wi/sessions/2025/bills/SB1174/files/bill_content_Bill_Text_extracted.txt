Referred to Committee on Education.SB1174,1,31An Act to amend 118.01 (2) (d) 2. d. of the statutes; relating to: incorporating 2awareness about fentanyl and the use of an opioid antagonist into school 3district educational goals.Analysis by the Legislative Reference BureauCurrent law specifies educational goals and expectations for school boards in each of the following categories: academic skills and knowledge, vocational skills, citizenship, and personal development. To satisfy the personal development educational goals, each school board must provide an instructional program on personal development that incudes specific objectives, including providing pupils with awareness about drug abuse and prevention.
