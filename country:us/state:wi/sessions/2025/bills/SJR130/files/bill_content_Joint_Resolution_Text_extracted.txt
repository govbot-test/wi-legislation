andSJR130,1,65Whereas, the presence of Wisconsin athletes at the Winter Games reflects the 6state’s strong tradition of winter sportsmanship; andSJR130,1,97Whereas, several Wisconsinites represent the United States on cross-country 8skis, including Deedra Irwin (Pulaski) and Paul Schommer (Appleton) in biathlon 9and Ben Loomis (Eau Claire) in Nordic combined; andSJR130,2,43Whereas, various Olympians from other states were born, raised, or honed 4their skills in Wisconsin; andSJR130,2,75Whereas, Madison was the birthplace of cross-country skier Gus Schumacher 6and biathlete Joanne Reid, daughter of Wisconsinite and Olympian Beth Heiden 7Reid;
