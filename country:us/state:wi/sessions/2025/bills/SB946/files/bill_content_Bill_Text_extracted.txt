Referred to Committee on Licensing, Regulatory Reform, State and Federal Affairs.SB946,1,31An Act to renumber and amend 175.37 (1); to amend 175.37 (title) and 2175.37 (2); to create 175.37 (1) (b) of the statutes; relating to: containers or 3trigger locks provided at a firearm sale and providing a penalty.Analysis by the Legislative Reference BureauThis bill requires a person who commercially sells or transfers a firearm to provide the purchaser or transferee with either a secure, lockable container that is designed to store a firearm or a trigger lock for the firearm. A seller or transferor who violates this requirement is subject to a fine of up to $500 or imprisonment for up to 30 days, or both.Because this bill creates a new crime or revises a penalty for an existing crime, the Joint Review Committee on Criminal Penalties may be requested to prepare a report.The people of the state of Wisconsin, represented in senate and assembly, do enact as follows:SB946,14Section 1. 175.37 (title) of the statutes is amended to read:SB946,1,55175.37 (title) Warning Requirements whenever transferring a firearm.SB946,21Section 2. 175.37 (1) of the statutes is renumbered 175.37 (1) (intro.) and 2amended to read:SB946,2,53175.37 (1) (intro.)
