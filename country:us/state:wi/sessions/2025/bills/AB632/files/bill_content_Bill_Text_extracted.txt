Referred to Committee on Environment.AB632,1,21An Act to amend 293.49 (1) (a) (intro.); to create 293.50 of the statutes; 2relating to: issuance of permits for the mining of sulfide ore bodies.Analysis by the Legislative Reference BureauCurrent law prohibits mining for nonferrous metallic minerals without a permit issued by the Department of Natural Resources. This bill prohibits DNR from issuing a permit for the mining of a sulfide ore body until DNR determines that a mine has operated in a similar sulfide ore body in the United States or Canada for at least 10 years without polluting groundwater or surface water and that a mine that operated in a similar sulfide ore body in the United States or Canada has been closed for at least 10 years without polluting groundwater or surface water.The people of the state of Wisconsin, represented in senate and assembly, do enact as follows:AB632,13Section 1. 293.49 (1) (a) (intro.) of the statutes is amended to read:AB632,2,24293.49 (1) (a) (intro.) Except as provided in sub. (2) and s. 293.50 and except 5with respect to property specified in s. 41.41 (11), within 90 days of the completion 1of the public hearing record, the department shall issue the mining permit if it 2finds:AB632,23Section 2. 293.50 of the statutes is created to read:AB632,2,64293.50 Moratorium on issuance of permits for mining of sulfide ore 5bodies. (1) In this section, “sulfide ore body” means a mineral deposit in which 6metals are mixed with sulfide minerals.AB632,2,97(2) Beginning on the effective date of this subsection .... [LRB inserts date], 8the department may not issue a permit under s. 293.49 for the mining of a sulfide 9ore body until all of the following conditions are satisfied:AB632,2,1410(a) The department determines, based on information provided by an 11applicant for a permit under s. 293.49, that a mining operation has operated in a 12sulfide ore body of similar geological characteristics in the United States or Canada 13for at least 10 years without the pollution of groundwater or surface water from acid 14drainage at the tailings site or at the mine site or from the release of heavy metals.AB632,2,2015(b) The department determines, based on information provided by an 16applicant for a permit under s. 293.49, that a mining operation that operated in a 17sulfide ore body of similar geological characteristics in the United States or Canada 18has been closed for at least 10 years without the pollution of groundwater or surface 19water from acid drainage at the tailings site or at the mine site or from the release 20of heavy metals.AB632,2,2221(3) This section applies without regard to the date of submission of the permit 22application.AB632,2,2323(end) Down Down /2025/related/proposals/ab632 true proposaltext /2025/related/proposals/ab632 proposaltext/2025/REG/AB632 proposaltext/2025/REG/AB632 section true Menu»2025»Related Documents»Proposal Text»AB632: Bill Text × Details for PDF view Link (Permanent link) Bookmark this location View toggle Go to top of document Search in this chapter Search in this section Search in this agency Search in this chapter group Search in this chapter Search in this section Cross references for section Acts affecting this section References to this 1970 Statutes Annotations Appellate Court Citations Administrative Code Index Reference lines Clear highlighting
