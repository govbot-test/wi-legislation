andWhereas, the U.S. Marine Corps thus celebrates November 10 as its official birthday; andWhereas, November 10, 2025, will be the U.S. Marine Corps’s 250th birthday; andWhereas, in celebration of this historic date, we recognize the U.S. Marine Corps for its valiant and unwavering service to our country, constitution, and liberties for the past 250 years; andWhereas, over 165,000 active-duty marines are currently stationed around the world to courageously defend our nation with unparalleled devotion; andWhereas, hundreds of marines have trained at Fort McCoy in Monroe County, where many hone their skills in live-fire exercises, medical training, and other activities, while some brave few demonstrate their remarkable grit in the Cold Weather Operations Course; andWhereas, every marine upholds and honors the U.S. Marine Corps’s motto of Semper Fidelis, “always faithful,” in their service to our nation and ideals; andWhereas, the people of Wisconsin sincerely appreciate the U.S. Marine Corps’s marines, veterans, and civilians for their sacrifices made in the name of the United States; andWhereas, in recognition of its uncompromising bravery and patriotism, the U.S. Marine Corps has our utmost confidence; now, therefore, be itResolved by the assembly, the senate concurring, That the Wisconsin Legislature celebrates the U.S. Marine Corps’s 250th birthday and recognizes its great history of service and achievement.
