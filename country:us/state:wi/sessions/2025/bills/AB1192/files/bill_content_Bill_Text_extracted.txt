Referred to Committee on Housing and Real Estate.AB1192,1,41An Act to create 20.490 (7), 234.47 and 710.15 (5w) of the statutes; relating 2to: penalties imposed on owners of mobile or manufactured home 3communities, grants for mobile homes and manufactured homes, and making 4an appropriation.Analysis by the Legislative Reference BureauUnder this bill, the Department of Safety and Professional Services may impose a $5,000 forfeiture on the owner of a mobile or manufactured home community for each year in which the owner violates any requirement under current law regarding the regulation of such communities, including reporting requirements. Under the bill, any unpaid forfeiture, assessment, or fee owed by the community owner creates a lien on the community property that continues until released and discharged by DSPS.Under the bill, the revenue received from the forfeitures is credited to the Wisconsin Housing and Economic Development Authority to provide grants to mobile or manufactured home community owners for the purpose of bringing the communities into compliance with licensing standards and owners of mobile homes or manufactured homes for the purpose of repairing, renovating, and improving such homes.For further information see the state fiscal estimate, which will be printed as an appendix to this bill.The people of the state of Wisconsin, represented in senate and assembly, do enact as follows:AB1192,11Section 1. 20.490 (7) of the statutes is created to read:AB1192,2,5220.490 (7) Mobile homes and manufactured homes. (a) Grants for mobile 3homes and manufactured homes. All moneys received under s. 710.15 (5w) for the 4purposes of awarding grants to owners of mobile or manufactured home 5communities and owners of mobile homes or manufactured homes.AB1192,26Section 2. 234.47 of the statutes is created to read:AB1192,2,87234.47 Mobile homes and manufactured homes. (1) Definitions. In 8this section:AB1192,2,99(a) “Manufactured home” has the meaning given in s. 101.91 (2).AB1192,2,1110(b) “Mobile home” has the meaning given in s. 101.91 (10), but does not 11include a recreational vehicle, as defined in s. 340.01 (48r).AB1192,2,1312(c) “Mobile or manufactured home community” has the meaning given for 13“community” in s. 710.15 (1) (ad).AB1192,2,1814(2) Grants. (a) The authority may provide grants to owners of mobile or 15manufactured home communities for the purpose of bringing the mobile or 16manufactured home communities into compliance with licensing standards. The 17authority may also provide grants to owners of mobile homes or manufactured 18homes for the purpose of repairing, renovating, and improving such homes.AB1192,319Section 3. 710.15 (5w) of the statutes is created to read:AB1192,3,920710.15 (5w) Penalties. (a) Any community owner who violates a 1requirement under this section, including a reporting requirement, may be subject 2to a $5,000 forfeiture for each year, or part thereof, in which the community owner 3is in violation of a requirement under this section. The department of safety and 4professional services shall impose and collect the forfeitures under this subsection. 5An unpaid forfeiture under this subsection and any unpaid assessments and fees 6owed by the community owner creates a lien that continues until released and 7discharged by the department. Forfeitures paid under this subsection shall be 8credited to the appropriation under s. 20.490 (7) (a) to be used for the purposes 9under s. 234.47.AB1192,3,1010(end) Down Down /2025/related/proposals/ab1192 true proposaltext /2025/related/proposals/ab1192 proposaltext/2025/REG/AB1192 proposaltext/2025/REG/AB1192 section true Menu»2025»Related Documents»Proposal Text»AB1192: Bill Text × Details for PDF view Link (Permanent link) Bookmark this location View toggle Go to top of document Search in this chapter Search in this section Search in this agency Search in this chapter group Search in this chapter Search in this section Cross references for section Acts affecting this section References to this 1970 Statutes Annotations Appellate Court Citations Administrative Code Index Reference lines Clear highlighting
