2025 ASSEMBLY BILL 925
January 28, 2026 - Introduced by Law Revision Committee. Referred to Committee on Rules.
AB925,1,2
1An Act; relating to: revising various provisions of the statutes for the purpose
2of making corrections and reconciling conflicts (Correction Bill).
Analysis by the Legislative Reference Bureau
This correction bill, prepared by the Legislative Reference Bureau under s. 13.92 (1) (bm) 1. and 2. and (2) (i) and (L), stats., is explained in the Notes in the body of the bill.
