Referred to Committee on Workforce Development, Labor, and Integrated Employment.AB650,1,31An Act to amend 20.445 (1) (aL) of the statutes; relating to: funding for 2identity verification under the unemployment insurance law and making an 3appropriation.Analysis by the Legislative Reference BureauCurrent law appropriates funding to the Department of Workforce Development for various costs associated with drug testing under the unemployment insurance law, with the unspent funds being directed to the unemployment program integrity fund at the end of every fiscal year.
