Referred to Committee on Insurance, Housing, Rural Issues and Forestry.SB1105,1,31An Act to amend 49.46 (2) (b) 1. (intro.); to create 49.43 (2x) and 49.46 (2) (bo) 2of the statutes; relating to: dental services for pregnant persons under the 3Medical Assistance program.Analysis by the Legislative Reference BureauUnder current law, certain basic dentists’ services provided to Medical Assistance recipients are covered under the Medical Assistance program. This bill provides Medical Assistance coverage for those same services if provided by a dentist or dental therapist.The bill also requires the Department of Health Services to ensure that a Medical Assistance recipient who is pregnant has the opportunity to see a dentist or dental therapist within 30 days of being referred for dental services under the Medical Assistance program. A dental therapist is an individual who is licensed to engage in the limited practice of dentistry. The Medical Assistance program is a joint state and federal program that provides health services to individuals who have limited financial resources.For further information see the state fiscal estimate, which will be printed as an appendix to this bill.The people of the state of Wisconsin, represented in senate and assembly, do enact as follows:SB1105,11Section 1. 49.43 (2x) of the statutes is created to read:SB1105,2,3249.43 (2x) “Dental therapist” means an individual licensed under s. 447.04 3(1m).SB1105,24Section 2. 49.46 (2) (b) 1.
