Referred to Committee on Judiciary and Public Safety.SB1166,1,31An Act to renumber 66.0511 (1) (a); to create 66.0511 (1) (ac), 66.0511 (1) (aj) 2and 66.0511 (5) of the statutes; relating to: use of autonomous devices 3equipped with weapons by law enforcement agencies.Analysis by the Legislative Reference BureauThis bill prohibits law enforcement agencies from using autonomous devices that are equipped with dangerous weapons. An “autonomous device” is defined as a device that is capable of operating without active control by a human operator.The people of the state of Wisconsin, represented in senate and assembly, do enact as follows:SB1166,14Section 1. 66.0511 (1) (a) of the statutes is renumbered 66.0511 (1) (ag).SB1166,25Section 2. 66.0511 (1) (ac) of the statutes is created to read:SB1166,1,7666.0511 (1) (ac) “Autonomous device” means a device that is capable of 7operating without active control by a human operator.SB1166,38Section 3. 66.0511 (1) (aj) of the statutes is created to read:SB1166,2,1166.0511 (1) (aj) “Dangerous weapon” has the meaning given in s. 939.22 (10).SB1166,42Section 4. 66.0511 (5) of the statutes is created to read:SB1166,2,4366.0511 (5) Use of autonomous devices.
