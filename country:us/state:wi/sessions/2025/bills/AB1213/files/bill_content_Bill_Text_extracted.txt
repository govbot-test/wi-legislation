Referred to Committee on Agriculture.AB1213,1,31An Act to create 20.115 (4) (ds) and 93.54 of the statutes; relating to: a dairy 2agriculture resilience investment now grant pilot program, granting rule-3making authority, and making an appropriation.Analysis by the Legislative Reference BureauThis bill requires the Department of Agriculture, Trade and Consumer Protection to create a Dairy Agriculture Resilience Investment Now grant pilot program, under which DATCP is required to provide grants to dairy producers with fewer than 1,000 head of milking cows in order for the dairy producers to undertake projects designed to improve their operational efficiency and resilience.
