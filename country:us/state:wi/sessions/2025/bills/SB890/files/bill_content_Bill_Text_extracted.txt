Referred to Committee on Agriculture and Revenue.SB890,1,31An Act to renumber 71.613 (3) (d) 1.; to create 71.613 (3) (am), 71.613 (3) (d) 21e. and 71.613 (3) (d) 1m. of the statutes; relating to: eligibility for farmland 3preservation tax credits.Analysis by the Legislative Reference BureauThis bill disallows a person from claiming the farmland preservation tax credit for any part of the claimant’s qualifying acres on which a photovoltaic solar energy system that is not an integral part of or incidental to an agricultural use. Under current law, a person may claim an individual income or corporate income and franchise tax credit for the person’s land engaged in an agricultural use, if the land is covered by a farmland preservation agreement or located in a farmland preservation zoning district, or both.For further information see the state fiscal estimate, which will be printed as an appendix to this bill.The people of the state of Wisconsin, represented in senate and assembly, do enact as follows:SB890,14Section 1. 71.613 (3) (am) of the statutes is created to read:SB890,2,3571.613 (3) (am) No credit may be allowed under this section for any part of a 1claimant’s qualifying acres on which a photovoltaic solar energy system, as defined 2in s. 13.48 (2) (h) 1. d., that is not an accessory use, as defined in s. 91.01 (1), is 3located during any part of the taxable year.SB890,24Section 2. 71.613 (3) (d) 1. of the statutes is renumbered 71.613 (3) (d) 1s.SB890,35Section 3. 71.613 (3) (d) 1e. of the statutes is created to read:SB890,2,6671.613 (3) (d) 1e. The number of the claimant’s qualifying acres.SB890,47Section 4. 71.613 (3) (d) 1m. of the statutes is created to read:SB890,2,10871.613 (3) (d) 1m.
