Referred to Committee on Senate Organization.SB909,1,41An Act to amend 203.02 (3) of the statutes; relating to: correcting a cross-2reference related to enforcement of regulations applicable to earned wage 3access service providers (suggested as remedial legislation by the Department 4of Financial Institutions).Analysis by the Legislative Reference BureauThis bill corrects a statutory cross-reference related to the enforcement authority of the Division of Banking (division) in the Department of Financial Institutions.Under current law, the division licenses and regulates providers of earned wage access services. The division also licenses and regulates money transmitters.The bill corrects a statutory cross-reference in a provision specifying that the division has the same power to conduct hearings, take testimony, and secure evidence in regulating earned wage access service providers that it has in regulating money transmitters.For further information, see the Notes provided by the Law Revision Committee of the Joint Legislative Council.The people of the state of Wisconsin, represented in senate and assembly, do enact as follows:Law Revision Committee prefatory note: This bill is a remedial legislation proposal, requested by the Department of Financial Institutions and introduced by the Law Revision Committee under s. 13.83 (1) (c) 4. and 5., stats. After careful consideration of the various provisions of the bill, the Law Revision Committee has determined that this bill makes minor substantive changes in the statutes, and that these changes are desirable as a matter of public policy.SB909,11Section 1. 203.02 (3) of the statutes is amended to read:SB909,2,32203.02 (3) The division has the same power to conduct hearings, take 3testimony, and secure evidence as is provided in ss. 217.17 and 217.18 s. 217.11.Note: This Section deletes cross-references to two statutory sections that were recodified and replaces them with the updated statutory citation.SB909,2,44(end) Down Down /2025/related/proposals/sb909 true proposaltext /2025/related/proposals/sb909 proposaltext/2025/REG/SB909 proposaltext/2025/REG/SB909 section true Menu»2025»Related Documents»Proposal Text»SB909: Bill Text × Details for PDF view Link (Permanent link) Bookmark this location View toggle Go to top of document Search in this chapter Search in this section Search in this agency Search in this chapter group Search in this chapter Search in this section Cross references for section Acts affecting this section References to this 1970 Statutes Annotations Appellate Court Citations Administrative Code Index Reference lines Clear highlighting
