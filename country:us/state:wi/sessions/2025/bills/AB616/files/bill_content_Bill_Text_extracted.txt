Referred to Committee on Campaigns and Elections.AB616,1,21An Act relating to: funding for reimbursements to municipalities for required 2office hours for in-person absentee voting and making an appropriation.Analysis by the Legislative Reference BureauThis bill provides funding of up to $10,000,000 to reimburse municipalities for a certain number of hours the municipal clerk’s office or alternate absentee ballot site may be required by law to remain open for in-person absentee voting at the election held on the first Tuesday in April each year or the November general election.For further information see the state fiscal estimate, which will be printed as an appendix to this bill.The people of the state of Wisconsin, represented in senate and assembly, do enact as follows:AB616,13Section 1. Fiscal changes.AB616,2,54(1) Municipality reimbursements for in-person absentee voting office 5hours.
