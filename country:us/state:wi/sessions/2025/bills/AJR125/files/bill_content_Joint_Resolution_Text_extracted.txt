andAJR125,1,129Whereas, the Black Hawks played hard to an unforgettable victory in the 10State Championship Game at Camp Randall Stadium in Madison on November 21, 112025, where the team triumphed 17-15 after a hard-fought battle and a game-12winning field goal;
