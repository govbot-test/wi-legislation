The group must also ensure that the credit card or debit card verification value or code is required and verified at the time the contribution is made and that the billing address is verified. However, if the contribution is made by a U.S. citizen living outside of the United States, and the credit card or debit card billing address is not a location in the United States, the individual making the contribution must provide the mailing address of the location in the United States that the individual uses for voter registration purposes.Finally, any third-party group that solicits and accepts money via the Internet by credit card or debit card to make contributions to a political committee or conduit without requiring the credit card or debit card verification value or code or address, as provided under the bill, is subject to a forfeiture in an amount equal to the cumulative amount of such contributions.The people of the state of Wisconsin, represented in senate and assembly, do enact as follows:AB385-ASA1,11Section 1. 11.0103 (6) of the statutes is created to read:AB385-ASA1,2,6211.0103 (6) Online credit card contributions.
