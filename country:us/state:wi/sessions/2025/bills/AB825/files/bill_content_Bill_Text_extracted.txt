Referred to Committee on Education.AB825,1,41An Act to amend 118.40 (2r) (e) 2p. a.; to create 20.255 (2) (dq) and 115.449 of 2the statutes; relating to: a grant program to support parent and guardian 3volunteer programs in schools, granting rule-making authority, and making 4an appropriation.Analysis by the Legislative Reference BureauUnder this bill, the Department of Public Instruction must develop and implement a grant program, under which it awards grants to school districts, independent charter schools, and private schools participating in a parental choice program for the purpose of creating or supporting parent and guardian volunteer programs. The bill requires DPI to begin awarding these grants in the 2026-27 school year.For further information see the state and local fiscal estimate, which will be printed as an appendix to this bill.The people of the state of Wisconsin, represented in senate and assembly, do enact as follows:AB825,11Section 1. 20.005 (3) (schedule) of the statutes: at the appropriate place, 2insert the following amounts for the purposes indicated: - See PDF for table AB825,27Section 2. 20.255 (2) (dq) of the statutes is created to read:AB825,2,9820.255 (2) (dq) Volunteer programs; grants.
