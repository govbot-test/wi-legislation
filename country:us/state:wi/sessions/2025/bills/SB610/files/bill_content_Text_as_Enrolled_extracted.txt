to create 961.49 (1m) (e) of the statutes; relating to: increasing the maximum penalty for certain controlled substances offenses if the violation occurs near a homeless shelter and providing a penalty.The people of the state of Wisconsin, represented in senate and assembly, do enact as follows:SB610,1Section 1. 961.49 (1m) (e) of the statutes is created to read:961.49 (1m) (e) While the person is in a homeless shelter or while the person is on or within 1,000 feet of the premises of a homeless shelter if either the person knows or should have known that he or she is on or within 1,000 feet of the premises of a homeless shelter or the homeless shelter is readily recognizable as a homeless shelter.SB610,2Section 2. 961.49 (2m) of the statutes is amended to read:961.49 (2m) If any person violates s. 961.65 and, during the violation, the person intends to deliver or distribute methamphetamine or a controlled substance analog of methamphetamine under any of the circumstances listed under sub. (1m) (a), (b), (c), or (d), or (e), the maximum term of imprisonment for that crime is increased by 5 years.
