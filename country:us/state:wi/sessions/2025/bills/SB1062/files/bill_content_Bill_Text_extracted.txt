(intro.); to create 320.155 (3) (b) and 196.504 (2r) of the statutes; relating to: broadband line 4extension grants and making an appropriation.Analysis by the Legislative Reference BureauThis bill requires the Public Service Commission to make grants to residents of properties that are not served by a broadband service provider to assist in paying the customer costs associated with line extension necessary to connect broadband service to the properties. The bill creates an appropriation for the broadband line extension grants, and the maximum amount of a grant is $4,000.
