Referred to Committee on Transportation and Local Government.SB747,1,31An Act to create 20.435 (1) (fo) and 146.621 of the statutes; relating to: grants 2to match county investments in the health care, public health, and care 3provider workforce and making an appropriation.Analysis by the Legislative Reference BureauThis bill requires the Department of Health Services to award grants to each county to match any investments made by the county in the previous fiscal year in the recruitment, retention, mental health, trauma care and prevention, or wellness of the county’s health care, public health, child care, long-term care, and other care provider workforce. Under the bill, grants may not exceed an amount equal to $1.11 multiplied by the county’s population.For further information see the state and local fiscal estimate, which will be printed as an appendix to this bill.The people of the state of Wisconsin, represented in senate and assembly, do enact as follows:SB747,14Section 1. 20.435 (1) (fo) of the statutes is created to read:SB747,2,2120.435 (1) (fo) Workforce investment grants. A sum sufficient for grants to 2counties for workforce investment under s. 146.621.SB747,23Section 2. 146.621 of the statutes is created to read:SB747,2,114146.621 Workforce investment grants. Each fiscal year, the department 5shall provide grants to each county to match any investments made by the county in 6the previous fiscal year in the recruitment, retention, mental health, trauma care 7and prevention, or wellness of the county’s health care, public health, child care, 8long-term care, and other care provider workforce.
