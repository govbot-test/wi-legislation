Referred to Committee on Licensing, Regulatory Reform, State and Federal Affairs.SB846,1,31An Act to create 165.85 (4) (a) 2m. of the statutes; relating to: citizenship as 2a requirement to be employed as a law enforcement officer or tribal law 3enforcement officer.Analysis by the Legislative Reference BureauUnder current law, no person may be employed as a deputy sheriff of any county or police officer for any city, village, or town unless that person is a citizen of the United States.
