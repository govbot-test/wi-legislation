Referred to Committee on Judiciary and Public Safety.SB897,1,31An Act to renumber and amend 947.016 (3); to create 947.016 (3) (am) of the 2statutes; relating to: threats to health care providers and providing a 3penalty.Analysis by the Legislative Reference BureauUnder current law, it is a Class H felony to threaten a health care provider or a person who works in a health care facility under specific circumstances.This bill defines "health care provider" and "health care facility" to include certain workers, such as emergency medical technicians, who may not work at a health care facility. These definitions align with the definition of those terms under current battery provisions. The bill also provides that the felony penalty applies if the threat is in response to an action taken by a health care provider in his or her official capacity, regardless of whether the action occurred at a health care facility.Because this bill creates a new crime or revises a penalty for an existing crime, the Joint Review Committee on Criminal Penalties may be requested to prepare a report.The people of the state of Wisconsin, represented in senate and assembly, do enact as follows:SB897,11Section 1. 947.016 (3) of the statutes, as created by 2025 Wisconsin Act 24, is 2renumbered 947.016 (3) (bm) and amended to read:SB897,2,73947.016 (3) (bm) Health care providers and staff and their family members. 4The person threatens a current or former health care provider or a person who 5works or formerly worked in a health care facility or a family member of a current or 6former health care provider or a person who works or formerly worked in a health 7care facility under all of the following circumstances:SB897,2,1181. At the time of the threat, the person knows or should have known that the 9victim is or was formerly a health care provider or works or formerly worked in a 10health care facility or is a family member of the person who is or was formerly a 11health care provider or who works or formerly worked in a health care facility.SB897,2,15122. The threat is in response to an action occurring at the a health care facility 13or an action by a health care provider acting in his or her official capacity or by an 14official, employee, or agent of the a health care facility acting in his or her official 15capacity.SB897,216Section 2. 947.016 (3) (am) of the statutes is created to read:SB897,2,1717947.016 (3) (am) In this subsection:SB897,2,18181. “Health care facility” has the meaning given in s. 940.51 (12).SB897,2,19192. “Health care provider” has the meaning given in s. 940.51 (13).SB897,2,2020(end) Down Down /2025/related/proposals/sb897 true proposaltext /2025/related/proposals/sb897 proposaltext/2025/REG/SB897 proposaltext/2025/REG/SB897 section true Menu»2025»Related Documents»Proposal Text»SB897: Bill Text × Details for PDF view Link (Permanent link) Bookmark this location View toggle Go to top of document Search in this chapter Search in this section Search in this agency Search in this chapter group Search in this chapter Search in this section Cross references for section Acts affecting this section References to this 1970 Statutes Annotations Appellate Court Citations Administrative Code Index Reference lines Clear highlighting
