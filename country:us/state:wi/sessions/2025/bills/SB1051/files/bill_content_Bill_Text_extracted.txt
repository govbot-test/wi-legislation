Referred to Committee on Education.SB1051,1,31An Act to amend 118.21 (title); to create 118.21 (5) and 119.40 (3) of the 2statutes; relating to: establishing minimum salaries for public school 3teachers.Analysis by the Legislative Reference BureauThis bill requires a school board to pay a full-time teacher an annual salary in each school year that is not less than the annual salary paid to a state legislator for that school year (base salary). In addition, if a teacher has worked as a teacher for at least 10 school years, the bill requires a school board to pay the teacher an annual salary that is not less than the base salary for the school year plus $15,000. Finally, if a teacher has worked as a teacher for at least 20 school years, the bill requires a school board to pay the teacher an annual salary that is not less than $100,000.For further information see the local fiscal estimate, which will be printed as an appendix to this bill.The people of the state of Wisconsin, represented in senate and assembly, do enact as follows:SB1051,14Section 1. 118.21 (title) of the statutes is amended to read:SB1051,1,55118.21 (title) Teacher contracts; minimum salaries.SB1051,21Section 2. 118.21 (5) of the statutes is created to read:SB1051,2,42118.21 (5) (a) In this subsection, “annual legislator salary” means the annual 3salary of a state senator or representative to the assembly established under s. 420.923 (2) (b), whichever is greater, for a particular fiscal year.SB1051,2,75(b) A school board shall pay to a full-time teacher an annual salary that is not 6less than the annual legislator salary for the school year for which the teacher’s 7salary is paid.SB1051,2,108(c) A school board shall pay to a full-time teacher who has worked as a teacher 9for at least 10 school years an annual salary that is not less than an amount 10calculated as follows:SB1051,2,12111.
