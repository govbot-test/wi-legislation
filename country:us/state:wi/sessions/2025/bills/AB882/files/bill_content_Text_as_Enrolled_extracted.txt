to amend 88.90 (1); to create 82.03 (5) (ag) of the statutes; relating to: town authority over the water elevation of an artificial water body created by a dam and recourse due to damage caused by an obstructed waterway.The people of the state of Wisconsin, represented in senate and assembly, do enact as follows:AB882,1Section 1. 82.03 (5) (ag) of the statutes is created to read:82.03 (5) (ag) Notwithstanding s. 31.02 (2), the town board may prescribe the maximum water elevation for an artificial water body created by a dam that includes the use of a diversion pump and that is located in the town if the town board has determined that a certain water elevation of that water body causes the road bed of a highway in the town to be saturated.AB882,2Section 2. 82.03 (5) (c) of the statutes is renumbered 82.03 (5) (b) 3.AB882,3Section 3. 88.90 (1) of the statutes is amended to read:88.90 (1) Whenever any natural watercourse becomes obstructed so that the natural flow of water along the same is retarded by the negligent action of the owner, occupant, or person in charge of the land on which the obstruction is located, the owner or occupant of any lands damaged due to ground or surface water flooding caused by such obstruction may request the removal thereof by giving notice in writing to such owner, occupant, or person in charge of the land on which the obstruction is located.
