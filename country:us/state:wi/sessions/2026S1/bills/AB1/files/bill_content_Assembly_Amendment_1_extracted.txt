(c) may not receive a grant under subd. 1. 17if a business that operates at the individual’s residence or contiguously to the 18individual’s residence has been awarded a grant under sub. (4) (a) for repairs to the 19same residence.AB1-AA1,4,21203. [LRB inserts date], 13whichever is later.AB1-AA1,5,16142. The individual provides to the department of military affairs a receipt or 15other documentation, as determined by the department of military affairs, for any 16disaster-related eligible expenses for which the individual seeks a grant.AB1-AA1,5,21173. The individual signs and provides to the department of military affairs a 18signed affidavit committing to return to the department of military affairs any part 19of the grant that is or becomes duplicated by the individual’s receipt of insurance 20money or other source of disaster-related assistance that is not a loan awarded 21directly by the federal government.AB1-AA1,5,22224.
